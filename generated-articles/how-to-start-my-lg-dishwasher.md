## How To Start Your LG Dishwasher: A Simple Guide

Your kitchen is clean, dinner is done, and now it is time for the dishwasher to work its magic. LG dishwashers are efficient machines. They make daily cleanup easier. You just need to know the correct steps to operate them.

This guide will show you how to start your LG dishwasher without fuss. We will cover everything from proper loading to selecting the right wash cycle. You will also learn about common issues and how to fix them. My goal is to help you get your dishes sparkling clean every time.

### Takeaway

*   Properly load dishes for best cleaning.
*   Choose the correct wash cycle for your load.
*   Ensure the door latches securely before starting.
*   Use appropriate detergent and rinse aid.
*   Troubleshoot basic issues like power or door problems.

To start your LG dishwasher, load dishes correctly, add detergent, select your desired wash cycle and options, close the door firmly until it latches, and press the "Start" or "Start/Pause" button. The dishwasher will then begin its wash program.

### Preparing Your LG Dishwasher for a Cycle

Getting ready for a wash cycle is more than just throwing dishes in. Proper preparation ensures optimal cleaning results. It also helps your LG dishwasher perform its best. Taking a few moments to prepare can prevent common issues.

First, scrape large food particles off your plates. You do not need to pre-rinse most dishes thoroughly. Modern LG dishwashers handle food residue well. Pre-rinsing wastes water and can reduce detergent effectiveness. Just remove solid bits of food that could clog the drain or spray arms.
