### Popular Carpet Types and Their Price Points in Stuart

You have many choices for carpet types in Stuart, FL. Each type offers different benefits and comes at a unique price point. Knowing these differences helps you select the best option for your home and budget. I will walk you through the most common choices.

**Nylon carpet** is a popular choice for high-traffic areas. It is known for its excellent durability and resilience. Nylon resists crushing and matting well. It also offers good stain resistance, especially with treatments. Prices for nylon carpet typically range from $3 to $8 per square foot for materials. This makes it a great value for long-term use.

**Polyester (PET) carpet** is a good mid-range option. It feels soft underfoot and offers vibrant colors. Polyester provides good stain resistance, especially against water-soluble stains. It is also often made from recycled materials, appealing to eco-conscious buyers. Material costs for polyester usually fall between $2.50 and $6 per square foot.

**Olefin (polypropylene) carpet** is a budget-friendly choice. It is highly resistant to moisture, mold, and mildew. This makes it suitable for basements or outdoor areas. However, olefin is less resilient than nylon and can mat down in heavy traffic. Expect to pay $2 to $4.50 per square foot for olefin materials.

**Wool carpet** is a luxurious and natural option. It is incredibly soft, durable, and naturally stain-resistant. Wool provides excellent insulation and is fire-resistant. However, it is also the most expensive option. Wool carpet materials typically cost $5 to $12 or more per square foot. This is a premium choice for high-end homes.

**Triexta carpet** is a newer synthetic fiber. It combines the durability of nylon with the stain resistance of polyester. Triexta is often made from renewable resources. It offers exceptional softness and durability. Material costs usually range from $3.50 to $7 per square foot. This carpet type offers a great blend of features.

Choosing the right type means balancing cost, durability, and aesthetics. Consider where the carpet will be installed. Think about the amount of foot traffic. Also, think about your family's lifestyle. Your Stuart, FL, carpet store can provide samples and expert advice.
