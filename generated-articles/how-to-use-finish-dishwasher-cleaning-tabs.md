### Step-by-Step Guide: How To Use Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs

Using Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs is a straightforward process. Following these steps ensures you get the best cleaning results. It is important to prepare your dishwasher first. This preparation maximizes the tab's effectiveness.

#### 1. Empty Your Dishwasher Completely
Before you start, make sure your dishwasher is empty. Remove all dishes, silverware, and any large food scraps. The cleaning tab needs to work on the machine's internal parts, not on dirty dishes. Running a cleaning cycle with dishes inside can actually make them dirtier or leave residue.

#### 2. Choose the Right Place for the Tab
For Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs (the solid tablet form), you typically place them in the detergent dispenser. This is the small compartment where you usually put your regular dishwashing detergent. The dispenser will open at the correct time in the cycle, releasing the cleaner. If you are using a Finish liquid dishwasher cleaner in a bottle, you would hang it upside down from the bottom rack, following its specific instructions. However, for tabs, the dispenser is the spot. This is a common question, so knowing [how to use Finish dishwasher cleaner tablets](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-use-finish-dishwasher-cleaner-tablets) is very helpful.

#### 3. Select a Hot Water Cycle
Close the dispenser door securely. Then, choose the hottest wash cycle available on your dishwasher. High temperatures help the cleaning tab dissolve thoroughly and activate its powerful cleaning agents. Cycles like "Intensive," "Heavy Duty," or "Sanitize" are good choices. A longer cycle with hot water ensures the cleaner has ample time to work on stubborn grime.

#### 4. Start the Dishwasher
Start your dishwasher. Let it complete the full cycle. Do not interrupt the cycle once it begins. The cleaning process typically takes as long as a normal hot wash cycle.

#### 5. After the Cycle
Once the cycle finishes, your dishwasher should be visibly cleaner and smell fresher. You do not need to do anything further immediately. The machine is ready for its next load of dishes. I often open the door slightly to let it air dry and ensure any remaining moisture escapes, which further helps with freshness. Sometimes, I may run a quick rinse cycle afterwards if I want to be extra sure, but it's usually not necessary.

First, consider the water temperature. As mentioned, hot water is crucial. The hotter the water, the better the cleaning agents in the tab work. Next, think about your dishwasher's filter. While cleaning tabs clean the internal parts, manually cleaning your filter periodically is also wise. A clogged filter can prevent water from circulating properly. It can trap food particles that lead to odors. I always remove and rinse my filter every few months. This practice, combined with cleaning tabs, provides a comprehensive clean. Water and detergent need space to circulate. Maintaining proper loading habits between cleaning cycles keeps your dishwasher in better condition. This reduces the rate of grime buildup. Following these simple steps will help your Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs perform at their peak. It will leave your machine spotless and ready for action.

### Common Mistakes to Avoid When Using Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs

Using Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs is simple, but some common errors can reduce their effectiveness. Knowing these mistakes helps you achieve the best possible clean for your appliance. Avoiding these pitfalls ensures you get the most value from your cleaning product.

One common mistake is running the cleaning cycle with dishes inside. As mentioned, the cleaning tab is for the machine, not for your dinnerware. The powerful cleaning agents are designed to strip away buildup from internal components. These chemicals are not formulated for food-safe dish cleaning. Always run the cleaning tab cycle with an empty dishwasher. This ensures the cleaner can work directly on the machine's surfaces.

Another error is not using a hot enough water cycle. Some people might opt for an eco-friendly or quick wash cycle. These cycles often use cooler water temperatures. Finish cleaning tabs work best with very hot water. Hot water helps dissolve the tab completely and activates its grease-fighting and limescale-removing properties. Always select the hottest, longest cycle available on your machine. This ensures optimal cleaning power.

Some users forget to check if the tab dissolved completely. After the cycle, quickly check the detergent dispenser. If any residue remains, it means the tab did not fully activate. This could be due to a short cycle, insufficient water temperature, or a dispenser issue. If this happens, you might need to run another cycle or manually remove the residue. Ensure the dispenser door opens freely during the cycle. Sometimes, items in the bottom rack can block it. Paying attention to these details will make your Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs more effective. Also, understanding [do you take Finish dishwasher tablets out of the plastic](https://homeessentialsguide.com/do-you-take-finish-dishwasher-tablets-out-of-the-plastic) is important, as some cleaner tabs, unlike detergent tabs, may not require wrapper removal. Always check the packaging instructions.

### FAQ Section

#### How often should I use Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs?
I recommend using Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs once a month. This regular schedule prevents the buildup of grease, limescale, and odor-causing residue. For homes with very hard water or heavy dishwasher use, you might consider cleaning every three weeks for optimal results. Consistent monthly cleaning keeps your machine in peak condition.

#### Can I use Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs with dishes inside?
No, you should never use Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs with dishes inside your machine. These tabs contain powerful cleaning agents meant for the appliance's internal parts, not for cleaning dinnerware. Running the cycle with an empty dishwasher ensures the cleaner works directly on grime and buildup without affecting your dishes.

#### Where do I put the Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tab?
Place the Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tab directly into the detergent dispenser of your dishwasher. This is the compartment where you usually put your regular dishwashing detergent. Close the dispenser door securely. If you use a liquid cleaner in a bottle, you would typically hang it from the top rack, but for tabs, the dispenser is the correct spot.

#### Which cycle should I use with Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs?
Always select the hottest and longest wash cycle available on your dishwasher when using a cleaning tab. Cycles like "Intensive," "Heavy Duty," or "Sanitize" are ideal. Hot water helps the cleaning tab dissolve completely and activates its powerful agents effectively. This ensures a thorough deep clean for your appliance.

#### Can Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs remove bad odors?
Yes, Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs are very effective at removing bad odors from your dishwasher. These odors often come from accumulated food particles, grease, and mold growth inside the machine. The cleaning tabs work to dissolve and flush away these odor-causing residues, leaving your dishwasher smelling fresh and clean after the cycle.

#### Do Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs clean the filter?
Finish Dishwasher Cleaning Tabs help clean various internal components, including parts of the filter system. They dissolve grease and limescale that might accumulate there. However, for a truly comprehensive clean, I still recommend manually removing and rinsing your dishwasher's filter periodically. This ensures all trapped food particles are cleared.
