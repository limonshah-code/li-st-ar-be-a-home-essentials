### Takeaway

*   **Act Fast:** Address fresh marshmallow spills immediately to prevent deep setting.
*   **Freeze First:** Use ice or a freezer pack to harden the marshmallow for easier removal.
*   **Scrape Gently:** Employ a dull knife or spoon to lift hardened marshmallow without damaging fibers.
*   **Clean Residue:** Use appropriate cleaning solutions for remaining stickiness or stains.
*   **Blot, Do Not Rub:** Always blot the area to avoid spreading the mess or damaging the carpet.
*   **Dry Thoroughly:** Ensure the cleaned area dries completely to prevent mold or mildew.

### How do you get marshmallow out of carpet effectively?

You can get marshmallow out of carpet by first hardening it with ice, then gently scraping it away. After removing the bulk, blot the area with a mild cleaning solution. This process tackles the sticky residue and any sugary stains. It restores your carpet's appearance.
