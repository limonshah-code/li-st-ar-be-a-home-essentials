## Returning Unopened Ikea Pillows: What You Need

Returning an unopened Ikea pillow is very simple. The process is straightforward if your pillow is still sealed in its original packaging. You need a few things to make your return quick and easy. Having these items ready saves time at the customer service desk.

First, always bring your proof of purchase. This is usually your original receipt. A receipt shows when and where you bought the pillow. It also proves the price you paid. If you used a credit card or debit card, the transaction often links to your account. This can sometimes help if you lose your paper receipt. Ikea staff can look up purchases using the card details.

Second, the pillow must be in its original, unused condition. This means it must be in the sealed plastic packaging it came in. The packaging should not be damaged or opened. ## FAQ Section

### What is Ikea's general return policy length?
Ikea allows returns for most items within 365 days of purchase. The item must be in new, unused condition, and in its original packaging. This generous policy gives customers ample time to decide if their purchase is suitable for their home needs.

### Can I return an Ikea pillow if it's been opened?
Generally, opened or used bedding, including pillows, cannot be returned for hygiene reasons. However, if the opened pillow is defective or fails to meet quality standards, you may still be able to return it. Always keep your proof of purchase.

### Do I need the original packaging to return an Ikea pillow?
For a smooth return, especially for unopened items, the original packaging is highly recommended. If the pillow is unused but out of its packaging, acceptance is at the store's discretion. For defective items, original packaging is less critical.

### How long does an Ikea pillow refund take?
If you return an item in-store with a receipt, refunds to the original payment method are often immediate or within 3-5 business days. Online returns or returns processed without a receipt (to store credit) may take longer to process and appear in your account.

### Can I exchange an Ikea pillow for a different one?
Yes, you can exchange an Ikea pillow. If you return an eligible pillow, you can use the refund amount to purchase a new pillow immediately. The exchange follows the same return policy rules regarding condition and proof of purchase.

### What if my Ikea pillow is defective?
If your Ikea pillow is defective, you can typically return it for a full refund or exchange, even if it has been opened or used. Bring your receipt and be prepared to explain or show the defect to the customer service staff. Ikea aims to ensure product quality.
