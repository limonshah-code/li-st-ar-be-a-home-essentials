## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does Finish Dishwasher Cleaner do?
Finish Dishwasher Cleaner removes grease and limescale buildup inside your dishwasher. Over time, food residue and hard water minerals accumulate on internal parts. This reduces cleaning performance and can cause odors. The cleaner works to dissolve these deposits, leaving your dishwasher fresh and operating more efficiently. It cleans hidden components you cannot reach by hand.

### How often should I use Finish Dishwasher Cleaner?
Most manufacturers recommend using Finish Dishwasher Cleaner once a month. Regular use prevents significant buildup of grease and limescale. This maintains your dishwasher's performance and prevents odors. If you live in an area with very hard water or use your dishwasher frequently, you might consider using it every two to three weeks.

### Can I use Finish Dishwasher Cleaner with dishes inside?
No, you should not use Finish Dishwasher Cleaner with dishes inside your dishwasher. The cleaner is a powerful descaler designed to clean the appliance itself. Always run the dishwasher cleaner on an empty cycle. This ensures the cleaning agents work effectively on the machine's components. It also prevents contact with your dishes.

### What is the difference between Finish Dishwasher Cleaner and Finish Dishwasher Detergent?
Finish Dishwasher Cleaner is a maintenance product. It cleans the *machine* itself, removing grease and limescale. Finish Dishwasher Detergent is for daily use. It cleans your *dishes* during a normal wash cycle. Both are essential for optimal dishwashing results. One cleans the appliance, the other cleans the items inside.

### Is Finish Dishwasher Cleaner safe for all dishwashers?
Finish Dishwasher Cleaner is generally safe for most major dishwasher brands. It is formulated to be compatible with common dishwasher materials. However, if you have a specific concern or a very old appliance, always consult your dishwasher's manual. Look for a section on recommended cleaning products. You can also check the Finish product label for any specific warnings.

### Where is the best place to put Finish Dishwasher Cleaner?
For liquid Finish Dishwasher Cleaner bottles, place the inverted bottle in the bottom rack of an empty dishwasher. For Finish Dishwasher Cleaner tablets, you can usually place them in the detergent dispenser or directly on the bottom of the empty dishwasher tub. Always follow the specific instructions on the product packaging for best results and proper usage.
