## Maintenance Tips for Your Maytag Refrigerator

Proper maintenance extends the life of any appliance, including a Maytag refrigerator. Simple steps can keep your unit running efficiently. Regular cleaning is essential. Wipe down interior surfaces and shelves with a mild soap solution. This prevents odors and keeps food fresh. Clean the exterior with a soft cloth and appropriate cleaner for the finish.

The condenser coils are vital for cooling. They collect dust and dirt over time. Dirty coils make the refrigerator work harder. This increases energy consumption. Clean the coils at least once a year. You can use a vacuum cleaner with a brush attachment. The coils are usually at the back or underneath the unit. Remember to unplug the refrigerator first.

Check door seals regularly for damage. Gaps in seals let cold air escape. This wastes energy and affects cooling. If seals are cracked or loose, replace them. Ensure the refrigerator is level. An unlevel refrigerator can cause doors to not seal properly. Learn [how to level your Maytag refrigerator](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-level-maytag-refrigerator) for optimal performance.

Replace water and air filters as recommended by Maytag. Water filters provide clean drinking water. Air filters reduce odors. These simple maintenance tasks help your Maytag refrigerator perform at its best. Following these tips helps ensure your Maytag refrigerator remains a good and reliable appliance for many years.
