## Mastering Spotless Dishes: How To Add Jet Dry To Your LG Dishwasher

Achieving sparkling, spot-free dishes straight from your dishwasher is a satisfying feeling. However, many people face the frustration of wet, streaky, or spotted glassware and cutlery. The secret often lies in a small but mighty ally: rinse aid, specifically Jet Dry. If you own an LG dishwasher, knowing how to add Jet Dry is a simple step that significantly improves drying performance and dish shine. This comprehensive guide helps you understand why rinse aid is vital, how to correctly add it to your LG dishwasher, and troubleshoot any common issues. We will cover everything from locating the dispenser to optimizing your dishwasher's drying cycles for perfect results every time.

### Takeaway

*   Locate your LG dishwasher's rinse aid dispenser on the inner door, next to the detergent compartment.
*   Open the dispenser cap by twisting or flipping it up, then carefully pour Jet Dry until the full indicator appears.
