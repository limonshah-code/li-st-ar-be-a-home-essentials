### Takeaway

*   Use specialized, low-tack, fabric-friendly adhesives or mounting clips for tape lights on carpet.
*   Prioritize safety by ensuring lights do not overheat and cables are not a trip hazard.
*   Clean carpet thoroughly before application for better adhesion.
*   Consider temporary mounting solutions or indirect lighting for long-term carpet protection.
*   Test any adhesive in an inconspicuous area first to prevent damage or residue.
