## Addressing Common Issues: When Your Tefal Air Fryer Needs Help

Even the best appliances can sometimes have issues. Knowing how to address common problems helps extend the life of your Tefal air fryer. Most problems are minor and easy to fix. My own experience with issues has been minimal.

One common concern is uneven cooking. If your food is not cooking consistently, check these points:
*   **Overcrowding:** Do not overfill the basket. This blocks air circulation. Cook in smaller batches if needed.
*   **Temperature/Time Settings:** Ensure you are using the correct settings for your food. Refer to your user manual or online guides.
*   **Stirring Arm (ActiFry models):** Make sure the stirring arm is installed correctly. It needs to turn freely.

Another issue could be the air fryer not turning on. First, check the power cord. Ensure it is plugged in firmly. Also, check your wall socket. Sometimes, a circuit breaker might trip. If your [Tefal air fryer is not working](https://homeessentialsguide.com/why-is-my-tefal-air-fryer-not-working), the problem might be a simple power issue.

Excessive smoke from the air fryer can be alarming. This usually happens due to fat buildup.
*   **Cleanliness:** Ensure the basket and pan are clean. Old food residue can burn.
*   **High-Fat Foods:** For very fatty foods, place a small amount of water at the bottom of the pan. This catches drips.
*   **Foil Usage:** Ensure foil is properly secured. It should not touch the heating element.

If the air fryer makes unusual noises, it might be due to a loose part. Turn off the appliance and check for anything rattling inside. Most fan noises are normal. However, grinding sounds might indicate a problem.

Regular cleaning and proper usage prevent many issues. Refer to your product manual for specific troubleshooting steps. Tefal's customer support can also help with more complex problems. With proper care, your Tefal air fryer will continue to perform well.
