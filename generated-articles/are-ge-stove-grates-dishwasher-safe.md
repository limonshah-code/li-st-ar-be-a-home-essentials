## Can Your GE Stove Grates Go in the Dishwasher?

Keeping your kitchen clean is important. GE stove grates can get very dirty with daily cooking. Many people wonder if they can simply put these grates in the dishwasher. This question, "Are GE stove grates dishwasher safe?", comes up often. Knowing the correct cleaning method saves you time and protects your appliance. This article explains if GE stove grates are dishwasher safe. We will look at different grate materials. You will learn the best ways to clean your GE stove grates.

### Takeaway:

*   Most GE cast iron stove grates are *not* dishwasher safe.
*   Porcelain-coated or enameled grates *might* be dishwasher safe. Always check your GE owner's manual.
*   Hand washing is often the safest and most effective method.
*   Proper care prevents damage and keeps grates looking new.
