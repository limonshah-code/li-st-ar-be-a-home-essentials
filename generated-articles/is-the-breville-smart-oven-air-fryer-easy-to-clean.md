## Deep Cleaning Your Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer

Sometimes, your Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer needs a deeper clean. This is for stubborn grease and baked-on food. Aim to deep clean every few weeks or months. This depends on how often you use it. Deep cleaning involves more steps than daily wiping.

It ensures all hidden spots are free of grime. This process helps maintain the oven's efficiency. It also extends its lifespan. Taking time for a thorough clean makes a big difference. It keeps your appliance in top shape.

### Cleaning the Interior Walls and Heating Elements

For the interior walls, make a paste of baking soda and water. Apply it to greasy or stained areas. Let it sit for 15-20 minutes. This helps loosen the grime. Then, use a damp cloth or soft sponge to wipe it away. For tougher spots, a gentle scrub might be needed. Avoid abrasive scrubbers. They can damage the non-stick coating.

Be careful around the heating elements. Do not apply water directly to them. Use a damp cloth to gently wipe off any visible crumbs or splatter. Do not scrub the heating elements hard. This can cause damage. Always ensure the oven is unplugged and cool before starting. Learning [how to deep clean an air fryer](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-deep-clean-air-fryer) involves these steps.

### Cleaning the Glass Door

The glass door can get splattered during cooking. Use a glass cleaner or a mixture of white vinegar and water. Spray it onto the glass. Wipe it with a clean, soft cloth. For stubborn grease, try the baking soda paste method. Apply, let sit, then wipe clean.

Ensure no cleaner drips into the oven interior. Always dry the glass door completely. A clean glass door lets you see your food cooking clearly. It also makes your Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer look better.

## Advanced Tips for Stubborn Stains and Odors

Even with regular cleaning, stubborn stains or odors can sometimes appear. This happens especially after cooking greasy foods. Do not worry. There are advanced tips to tackle these tough issues. These methods use simple household items.

They help restore your Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer to its clean, fresh state. These tips focus on natural solutions. They avoid harsh chemicals. This keeps your appliance safe for food preparation.

### Tackling Burnt-On Food and Grease

For burnt-on food, try a gentle scraper. Use a plastic or silicone scraper. This helps lift the crusty bits without scratching. Do not use metal tools. After scraping, apply a baking soda paste. Mix baking soda with a little water to form a thick paste. Spread it over the burnt areas. Let it sit for several hours, or even overnight. The baking soda helps to break down the grime.

Then, use a damp cloth or soft sponge to wipe it away. Repeat if necessary. For really tough grease, a solution of warm water and white vinegar can help. Spray it on and let it soak before wiping. This method is similar to how you would [clean the bottom of an oven](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-bottom-of-oven) when dealing with burnt spills.

### Removing Persistent Odors

Persistent odors often come from trapped grease or burnt food residue. First, ensure the oven is thoroughly clean. Use the deep cleaning steps for interior walls and accessories. If odors remain, try a natural deodorizer.

Place a bowl of white vinegar inside the cool, unplugged oven. Leave it overnight. Vinegar absorbs odors. You can also use a bowl of baking soda. This works similarly. For a fresh smell, wipe the interior with a lemon half. This leaves a pleasant citrus scent. Run the air fryer empty for 10-15 minutes on a low setting with good ventilation. This helps burn off any remaining smells.

## FAQs About Cleaning Your Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer

### Q1: Can I use oven cleaner on my Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer?

No, do not use harsh oven cleaners on your Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer. These cleaners contain strong chemicals. They can damage the non-stick interior coating. They may also corrode metal parts. Stick to mild dish soap, baking soda paste, or vinegar solutions. These are safer and effective for cleaning.

### Q2: How often should I deep clean my Breville air fryer oven?

You should deep clean your Breville air fryer oven every 1-3 months. This depends on how often you use it. If you cook greasy foods frequently, deep clean more often. Daily wiping and crumb tray emptying reduce the need for frequent deep cleaning.

### Q3: What if food is really stuck to the air fry basket?

If food is really stuck, soak the air fry basket. Use hot, soapy water. Let it soak for 30 minutes to an hour. This softens the food. Then, use a non-abrasive brush or sponge. You can also make a baking soda paste. Apply it and let it sit. This helps loosen stubborn bits.

### Q4: Is it safe to use water directly on the heating elements?

No, do not use water directly on the heating elements. This can damage them. They are electrical components. Always keep them dry. Instead, use a slightly damp cloth. Gently wipe away any loose crumbs or residue. Ensure the oven is unplugged and cool.

### Q5: Can I clean my Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer with a steam cleaner?

Using a steam cleaner is generally not recommended for the interior of the Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer. The high heat and moisture could damage internal components or coatings. Stick to manual cleaning methods with water, mild soap, and natural cleaning agents.

### Q6: How do I prevent smoke when cooking in my air fryer oven?

Prevent smoke by regularly cleaning your air fryer oven. Accumulated grease and food bits burn and cause smoke. Always empty the crumb tray. Clean the air fry basket and interior walls after each use. Use less oil when cooking. Use parchment paper or aluminum foil on the crumb tray for easy cleanup.
