## Setting Up and Caring for Your Emma Essential Mattress

### Initial Setup and Expansion

Your Emma Essential Mattress arrives conveniently compressed in a box. Once you unbox it, place the rolled mattress on your bed frame. Carefully cut away the plastic packaging. The mattress will begin to expand immediately. Give it some time to fully decompress.

Emma usually recommends allowing 24 to 72 hours for the mattress to reach its full size and firmness. During this time, the foam layers need to settle. It is normal to notice a slight "new mattress" smell, known as off-gassing, during this period. This smell is harmless and will dissipate. Ensure your room is well-ventilated to help it clear faster.

A sturdy and supportive bed frame is essential for the Emma Essential. A slatted base with slats no more than 3 inches apart works well. A solid platform base is also suitable. Avoid old or flimsy foundations, as they can cause sagging and void your warranty. The right foundation supports the mattress correctly.

### Maintaining Mattress Longevity

To ensure your Emma Essential Mattress lasts, some simple maintenance practices are necessary. First, rotate your mattress every 3 to 6 months. This helps distribute wear evenly and prevents permanent body impressions. Flipping is generally not required, as Emma mattresses are designed with specific top layers. I find rotation easy to remember.

Keep your mattress clean by using a mattress protector. This shield guards against spills, stains, and allergens. The Emma Essential often comes with a removable, washable cover. Check the care label for specific washing instructions. Regular cleaning of the cover helps maintain hygiene.

Avoid jumping on the mattress or allowing pets with sharp claws to use it. These activities can damage the foam layers. Make sure your bed frame provides continuous support across the entire mattress surface. Proper care prolongs the life of your mattress. It keeps it feeling fresh and supportive for years to come.
