## How To Clean A Hairbrush Video

Have you ever looked at your hairbrush and wondered, "How much gunk is actually in there?" I know I have. Your hairbrush touches your hair every day. It collects stray hairs, dust, dead skin cells, and product residue. A dirty brush can spread grime back onto your clean hair. It also becomes less effective at detangling or styling. Cleaning your hairbrush is a simple step you can take for better hair hygiene. This guide will walk you through the process, covering various brush types and ensuring a sparkling clean result. We will explain why it matters, what tools you need, and the best cleaning methods.
