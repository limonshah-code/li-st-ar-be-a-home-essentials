### FAQ Section

**Q1: Can I put my Ember Mug in the microwave?**
A1: No, absolutely not. The Ember Mug contains a battery and electronic components. Placing it in a microwave oven can cause severe damage. It can even lead to fires or explosions. Ember Mugs are designed to heat drinks themselves. They do not need external heating.

**Q2: What should I do if my Ember Mug accidentally gets wet?**
A2: If your Ember Mug gets wet, especially on the charging base or inside, dry it immediately. Turn it upside down to drain any water. Do not attempt to charge or use it. Let it air dry for several days. This helps prevent internal damage, but success is not guaranteed.

**Q3: How often should I clean my Ember Mug?**
A3: Clean your Ember Mug after every use. This prevents stains and bacteria growth. A quick rinse and wipe are usually enough. For a deeper clean, use warm soapy water with a soft sponge. Regular cleaning keeps your mug fresh and hygienic.

**Q4: Can I use any dish soap to clean my Ember Mug?**
A4: Yes, most standard dish soaps are fine. Choose a mild dish soap. Avoid abrasive cleaners or those with strong chemicals. These can damage the mug's finish or internal components. Gentle cleaning is always the best approach.

**Q5: What if coffee stains persist inside my Ember Mug?**
A5: For stubborn coffee stains, try a solution of baking soda and water. Make a paste and gently scrub the stain with a soft cloth. Let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing. This natural cleaner helps lift tough stains without harming the mug.
