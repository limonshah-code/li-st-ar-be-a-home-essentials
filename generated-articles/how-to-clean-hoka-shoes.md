## Keep Your Hokas Fresh: How To Clean Hoka Shoes Properly

Your Hoka shoes are more than just footwear. They are a crucial tool for your runs, walks, or daily comfort. Dirty shoes can affect their performance. Grime can also shorten their lifespan. Knowing how to clean Hoka shoes properly keeps them looking good. This care also helps them feel supportive. This guide offers easy steps to wash your Hokas. We cover removing mud and safe drying. Follow these tips to maintain your Hoka sneakers. This ensures maximum comfort and durability.

### Takeaway

*   **Hand wash your Hokas:** Avoid washing machines. This protects the cushioning.
