### Mister Coffee Not Turning On: Power and Connection Checks

My Mister Coffee maker sometimes refuses to power on. This is frustrating. A lack of power is a frequent reason for this issue. You need to check a few things before assuming the worst. Power problems are often simple to fix. Do not assume the machine is broken. Start with the basics. This can save you time and money.

### Wall Outlet and Plug Inspection
First, inspect the wall outlet where your coffee maker is plugged in. Is the plug firmly in the socket? Sometimes, a loose plug is the only problem. Push it in tightly. Try plugging another small appliance, like a lamp or phone charger, into the same outlet. This confirms if the outlet itself works. If the other appliance does not work, the problem is with your outlet, not the coffee maker. Consider moving the coffee maker to a different, working outlet.
