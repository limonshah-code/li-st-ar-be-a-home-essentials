Gluten is a protein compound. It exists in wheat, barley, and rye. For people with celiac disease, gluten causes damage to the small intestine. Others experience gluten sensitivity, leading to various uncomfortable symptoms. These conditions require strict avoidance of gluten.

Most people associate gluten with food items. However, gluten can appear in unexpected places. Personal care products, medications, and even cleaning supplies can contain gluten. Contact with gluten can trigger reactions in sensitive individuals. Accidental ingestion of residues is a concern with cleaning products used on dishes.
