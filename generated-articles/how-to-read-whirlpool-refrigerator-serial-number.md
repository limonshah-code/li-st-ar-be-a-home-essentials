## Decoding the Whirlpool Refrigerator Serial Number Format

This format encodes important information, especially the manufacturing date. Knowing this structure helps you extract the details you need.

A typical Whirlpool serial number will be a combination of letters and numbers. It usually starts with two letters, followed by numbers. For example, you might see something like "SR1234567" or "K812345678". The key to decoding the date often lies in a specific sequence within these characters.

Many Whirlpool appliances, including refrigerators, use a system where the first letter (or sometimes the second, depending on the older vs. newer format) denotes the year of manufacture. The following two numbers usually indicate the week of that year. For instance, if you see 'R23' in the relevant position, 'R' identifies the year, and '23' indicates the 23rd week of that year.
