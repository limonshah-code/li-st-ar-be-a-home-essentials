### Why Your Dishwasher Needs Regular Cleaning

Your dishwasher works hard every day, scrubbing away food and grease from your plates. Over time, this hard work takes a toll on the appliance itself. Food particles can escape the filter and build up in hidden areas. Grease residues may coat the interior walls and spray arms.

Hard water can leave behind mineral deposits, like limescale, on heating elements and spray jets. These buildups hinder performance. They can block water flow and reduce the effectiveness of your detergent. A dirty dishwasher also becomes a breeding ground for bacteria and mold, leading to unpleasant odors. You might notice your dishes do not come out as clean as before. This indicates it is time for a deep clean. Ensuring your dishwasher is clean means your dishes get a proper wash every time.
