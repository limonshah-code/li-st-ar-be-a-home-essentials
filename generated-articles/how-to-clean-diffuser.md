## Unlock Your Diffuser's Full Potential: The Ultimate Cleaning Guide

Your essential oil diffuser fills your home with soothing scents. Yet, like any well-used appliance, it needs regular cleaning. Essential oil residue and mineral deposits can build up over time. This buildup affects performance and can lead to hygiene problems. Proper cleaning keeps your diffuser working at its peak. It ensures you receive the full benefits of your essential oils. This comprehensive guide will show you precisely how to clean diffuser models of all types. We cover everything from quick daily rinses to thorough deep cleans.
