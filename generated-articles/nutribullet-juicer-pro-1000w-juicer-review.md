### Centrifugal vs. Cold Press

It is important to understand the difference between centrifugal and cold press (masticating) juicers.
*   **Centrifugal Juicers (like Nutribullet Juicer Pro 1000w):** These are fast. They use high-speed blades to grate produce. They then spin the pulp to extract juice. They are great for quick, daily juice. They are generally more affordable. They can produce more foam.
*   **Cold Press/Masticating Juicers:** These are slow. They crush and press produce slowly. They produce less foam. They are often better for leafy greens. They yield juice with a longer shelf life. They retain more sensitive enzymes. They are usually more expensive and slower to use.
