#### Performing a Hard Reset

A hard reset, also known as a power cycle, is often the first and most effective troubleshooting step for any electronic appliance. This process can clear minor software glitches or temporary electronic malfunctions. When your Beko washing machine display shows unusual text or symbols, performing a hard reset is a smart starting point. It is simple and does not require any tools.

To perform a hard reset, first, turn off your washing machine using its power button. Do not just pause the cycle; turn the machine completely off. Next, unplug the machine's power cord from the electrical outlet. This step is crucial. It ensures that all residual power drains from the system's capacitors. Wait for a minimum of 5 to 10 minutes. This waiting period allows the electronic components to fully discharge and reset.

After the waiting period, plug the washing machine back into the wall outlet. Turn the machine on. Observe the display. See if the unusual "Lj" or other text has disappeared. This simple action often resolves display anomalies. It can also fix issues like a frozen display or unresponsive buttons. If the "Lj" symbol was due to a temporary glitch, a hard reset will likely clear it. This allows you to resume your washing machine's operation.

#### Power Supply Check and Solutions

A stable and consistent power supply is vital for your Beko washing machine's proper operation. Erratic power can cause the display to show unusual symbols or to malfunction. If your washing machine's display is acting strangely, or if you suspect "Lj" might be related to a power issue, start by checking your power source.

First, ensure the washing machine is plugged directly into a wall outlet. Avoid using extension cords or power strips. These can sometimes limit the power supply or introduce inconsistencies. Check the outlet itself. You can test it by plugging in another appliance. If the other appliance works, the outlet is likely fine.

Next, check your home's circuit breaker box. Locate the breaker for the washing machine's outlet. Make sure it is in the "On" position. Sometimes, a breaker can trip, cutting power to the outlet. This is a common issue when an appliance like your [Beko washing machine keeps tripping the electric](https://homeessentialsguide.com/why-does-my-beko-washing-machine-keep-tripping-the-electric). If the breaker is tripped, reset it by flipping it off and then firmly back to the "On" position.

Finally, consider large fluctuations in your home's power. Power surges or dips can temporarily affect electronic components. If you experience frequent power issues, a surge protector designed for large appliances might be a worthwhile investment. This helps protect your washing machine's delicate electronics, including its display, from damage.

Checking these connections requires opening the machine's casing. This should only be done by someone with electrical knowledge.

#### Preventative Maintenance Tips

Beyond regular cleaning, several preventative maintenance tips can keep your Beko washing machine running optimally. These practices help avoid stress on the machine's components. They also reduce the likelihood of encountering perplexing display issues like "Lj."

First, avoid overloading the machine. Overloading puts excessive strain on the motor, drum, and suspension system. This can lead to premature wear and tear. It also impacts the machine's balance during the spin cycle. Always follow the manufacturer's recommended load capacity for each program.

Second, use the correct amount and type of detergent. Too much detergent can create excessive suds. This leaves residue in the machine and on your clothes. It can also cause issues with rinsing or even leaks. Use high-efficiency (HE) detergent if your machine requires it.

Third, ensure the machine is level. An unbalanced washing machine vibrates excessively. This can damage components over time. Use a spirit level to check if your machine is stable. Adjust the leveling feet as needed. This prevents strain on internal parts.

Lastly, give your washing machine breaks between heavy loads. This allows the motor and other components to cool down. It prevents overheating. Proper ventilation around the machine is also important. These simple steps contribute to the machine's longevity. They help maintain all its functions, including a clear and accurate display.
