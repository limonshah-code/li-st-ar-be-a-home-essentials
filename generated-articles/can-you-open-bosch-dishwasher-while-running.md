### Safety Precautions When Opening a Running Dishwasher

Even with Bosch’s safety features, taking precautions is wise. Hot water and steam are present inside the dishwasher. They can cause burns if you are not careful. Always approach the dishwasher cautiously when it is running.

Second, avoid putting your face directly over the opening. Lean back slightly as you open the door. This keeps your face away from the initial burst of steam. This is especially important during hotter parts of the cycle. Steam can cause painful burns to exposed skin.
