### Maximizing Longevity: Essential Maintenance for Your Frigidaire Refrigerator

Proper care helps your Frigidaire refrigerator last longer. Simple maintenance steps prevent common issues. They keep your appliance running at its best. Taking a few minutes each month can save you time and money.

First, keep the condenser coils clean. These coils are often at the back or bottom of the unit. Dust and pet hair can collect on them. Dirty coils make the refrigerator work harder. This wastes energy. Clean them twice a year with a vacuum cleaner brush. This simple step improves efficiency and extends compressor life.

Second, check door seals regularly. A tight seal keeps cold air inside. If seals are cracked or loose, cold air escapes. You can test them with a dollar bill. Close the door on the bill. If you can pull it out easily, the seal needs replacement. Also, ensure your refrigerator is level. An unlevel refrigerator can stress the cooling system. If you need to [turn on your Frigidaire refrigerator](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-turn-frigidaire-refrigerator-on) after moving it, ensure it sits properly.

Finally, change water filters as recommended. This maintains the quality of your drinking water and ice. Many Frigidaire models have an indicator light for this. Also, be aware of features like door locks. If you need to [unlock your Frigidaire refrigerator](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-unlock-my-frigidaire-refrigerator) for cleaning or access, refer to your manual. Regular attention to these details helps ensure years of trouble-free operation from your Frigidaire.
