Bursa's importance in textile arts goes back centuries. This city was the first capital of the Ottoman Empire. Its location was key along the historic Silk Road. The city quickly became famous for its silk products. It was also known for other fine textiles, including rugs. Weavers in Bursa developed advanced skills. The city was a place where artistry met commerce.
