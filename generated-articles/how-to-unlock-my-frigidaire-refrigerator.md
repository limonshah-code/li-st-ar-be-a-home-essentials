## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is my Frigidaire control panel locked?
Your Frigidaire control panel locks to prevent accidental changes to settings or unsupervised use by children. This feature is a safety measure. It can be activated by pressing a specific "Lock" or "Child Lock" button for a few seconds. Power fluctuations can also sometimes trigger the lock feature.

### How do I reset the lock on my Frigidaire?
To reset the lock on your Frigidaire, locate the "Lock" or "Child Lock" button on the control panel. Press and hold it for three to five seconds. The lock indicator light should turn off. If this does not work, unplug the refrigerator from the wall outlet for five minutes, then plug it back in.

### Can a power outage cause my Frigidaire to lock?
Yes, a power outage or a power surge can sometimes cause your Frigidaire refrigerator to enter a locked state. Electronic controls can become confused by sudden power interruptions. A quick power cycle, where you unplug and replug the unit, often resolves this.

### What if the lock button does not work?
If the lock button does not work, try a power cycle first. Unplug the refrigerator for at least five minutes, then plug it back in. Also, ensure the control panel is clean and free of moisture. If the button is physically damaged or the panel remains unresponsive, it may indicate a control board issue requiring professional service.

### Is child lock different from a control lock?
Often, a "child lock" is a type of "control lock." The child lock feature typically disables the entire control panel and dispenser to prevent children from interacting with the refrigerator. A general control lock might disable just the settings, sometimes leaving the dispenser active, depending on the model. Check your manual for specific details.

### How can I find my Frigidaire model number?
You can find your Frigidaire refrigerator's model number on a label inside the fresh food compartment. It is usually located on the upper left or right side wall, or sometimes behind the crisper drawers. It might also be on the back of the refrigerator or inside the freezer door.
