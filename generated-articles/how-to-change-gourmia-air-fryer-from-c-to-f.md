## Step-by-Step Guide to Changing Temperature Units

Switching your Gourmia air fryer from Celsius to Fahrenheit is usually straightforward. While specific button combinations might vary slightly by model, the underlying principle is similar. Most Gourmia air fryers have a dedicated method for this. Here are the most common ways to make the change:

1.  **Locate the Temperature Button:** On many Gourmia models, you will find a button labeled "TEMP" or an icon resembling a thermometer. This is often the key to changing units.
2.  **Press and Hold:** With the air fryer turned on (but not necessarily running a cooking cycle), press and hold the "TEMP" button. Hold it down for about 3 to 5 seconds.
3.  **Observe the Display:** After holding, the display should switch from 'C' to 'F' (or vice versa). You might see a brief flash before the unit changes.
4.  **Confirm the Change:** Once it changes, release the button. The air fryer should now display temperatures in Fahrenheit. Try setting a temperature to ensure it displays correctly.

If the "TEMP" button method does not work, try these alternatives:

*   **"M" or "Mode" Button:** Some Gourmia models use an "M" or "Mode" button. Press and hold this button for a few seconds. This can cycle through different settings, including temperature units.
*   **Temperature Up/Down Arrows:** On certain models, you might need to press and hold both the "Temp Up" and "Temp Down" arrows simultaneously. Try this for 3-5 seconds.
*   **Combination with Power Button:** In rare cases, a specific button combination with the power button might be needed. This is less common but worth considering if other methods fail.

Remember, the goal is to make a quick adjustment. Your air fryer is designed for user convenience. Once you find the correct button sequence, switching units becomes second nature. ## FAQ Section

### Why is my Gourmia air fryer in Celsius?
Your Gourmia air fryer may be in Celsius by default if it was manufactured for a market that uses the metric system, or simply as its factory setting. This is a common setup for many electronic appliances sold globally. You can usually change this setting easily through the control panel.

### Are all Gourmia air fryers the same for temperature change?
No, not all Gourmia air fryers are exactly the same. While many share similar methods for changing temperature units, specific button combinations or locations can vary by model. Newer models might have touchscreens, while older ones use physical buttons. Always check your specific model's manual if general methods do not work.

### What if the buttons don't work when I try to change units?
If your Gourmia air fryer buttons are unresponsive, first ensure the unit is powered on and plugged in. Try unplugging it for a minute to perform a hard reset, then plug it back in. Make sure you are holding the button for the required 3-5 seconds. If issues persist, there might be a minor internal fault.

### Can I reset my Gourmia air fryer?
Most Gourmia air fryers do not have a dedicated "reset" button. The common way to reset the unit is by unplugging it from the power outlet for a few minutes. This hard reset can often clear temporary glitches or unresponsive settings. After plugging it back in, try changing the temperature units again.

### Does changing C to F affect cooking performance?
Changing the display from Celsius to Fahrenheit does not affect the air fryer's cooking performance. It only changes how the temperature is displayed. The internal heating mechanism remains the same. This adjustment simply helps you follow recipes more accurately, ensuring your food cooks at the correct desired temperature.

### How do I know if my Gourmia air fryer successfully changed units?
After pressing the required buttons, observe the display. The 'C' next to the temperature reading should change to an 'F'. If you set a temperature, the display will show the number followed by 'F'. Some models might also beep or flash briefly to confirm the change.
