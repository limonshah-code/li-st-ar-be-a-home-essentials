### FAQ Section

**Q1: Is Breville a good brand for juicers?**
Yes, Breville is a highly respected brand for juicers. They are known for their high-quality construction, powerful motors, and efficient juice extraction. Customers often praise their durability and user-friendly designs. Breville juicers offer a great balance of performance and reliability.

**Q2: Can you put a whole lemon in a Breville juicer?**
No, you cannot put a whole lemon into a Breville citrus juicer. You must cut all citrus fruits in half before juicing. The reaming cone is designed to fit into the cut half of the fruit to extract the juice effectively. This is standard for most citrus juicers.

**Q3: How do you clean a Breville citrus juicer?**
Cleaning a Breville citrus juicer is easy. Most removable parts, like the reaming cone, filter, and juice collector, are dishwasher safe. For best results, rinse these parts immediately after use to prevent pulp from drying. You can also hand wash them quickly with warm soapy water.

**Q4: What is the difference between a citrus juicer and a regular juicer?**
A citrus juicer is specifically designed for juicing citrus fruits, which are cut in half and pressed onto a reaming cone. A regular juicer, often called a centrifugal or masticating juicer, handles a wider range of fruits and vegetables, usually requiring them to be fed through a chute whole or in larger pieces.

**Q5: Is freshly squeezed juice better than store-bought?**
Yes, freshly squeezed juice is generally better than store-bought juice. Fresh juice retains more vitamins, minerals, and enzymes that can degrade over time. It also contains no added sugars, preservatives, or artificial flavors often found in packaged juices. This makes it a healthier and more flavorful option.
