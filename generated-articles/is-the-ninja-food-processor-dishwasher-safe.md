### Never Submerge the Motor Base

This can damage the appliance permanently. It also poses a serious shock hazard. Never rinse it under the tap. Never put it in the dishwasher.

Clean the motor base with a damp cloth only. Make sure the cloth is not dripping wet. Wipe down the exterior surfaces carefully. If you spill liquid on the base, unplug it immediately. Wipe it dry completely before using it again. Always prioritize safety when cleaning electrical appliances.

### Avoiding Abrasive Cleaners and Scourers

While tough on food, some cleaning tools are too harsh for your food processor. Abrasive scrub brushes, steel wool, or scouring pads can scratch plastic surfaces. Use soft sponges or cloths for cleaning plastic components. Mild dish soap is usually sufficient. For stubborn stains, try soaking the part first. A paste made of baking soda and water can also work wonders. It removes stains without scratching. Gentle cleaning methods preserve the finish. They also maintain the hygiene of your appliance.

### Can I put the Ninja motor base in the dishwasher?

No, absolutely not. The Ninja motor base contains electrical components and is never dishwasher safe. Submerging it in water or placing it in the dishwasher will cause severe damage to the unit. It also creates a significant electrical hazard. Always clean the motor base by wiping it with a damp cloth only.

### How do I clean sticky residue from my Ninja food processor?

For sticky residue, start by pre-soaking the dishwasher-safe parts in warm, soapy water. If residue remains, create a paste with baking soda and a little water. Apply it to the affected area. Let it sit for a few minutes. Then, gently scrub with a soft brush or sponge. Rinse thoroughly afterwards.

### Are Ninja blades replaceable if they get dull?

Yes, Ninja offers replacement blade assemblies for many of their food processor models. Over time, blades can dull with use. You can often purchase new blade sets directly from Ninja's website or authorized retailers. Replacing dull blades helps maintain your food processor's efficiency and performance.

### What should I do if my plastic Ninja parts become cloudy?

Cloudiness on plastic parts can occur over time from dishwasher use or hard water. Try soaking the cloudy parts in a solution of equal parts white vinegar and water for about 30 minutes. Then, scrub gently with a soft sponge and rinse well. This can often help restore clarity. Hand washing may prevent future cloudiness.

### How often should I deep clean my Ninja food processor?

Regular cleaning after each use is essential for hygiene. For a deep clean, I recommend disassembling all removable parts and thoroughly cleaning seals and crevices at least once a month. If you use your food processor frequently for sticky or strong-smelling foods, deep clean it more often to prevent buildup and odors.
