## Understanding PTFE and PFOA in Non-Stick

To truly understand why a Gourmia air fryer being Teflon-free matters, we need to talk about PTFE and PFOA. Teflon is a brand name for Polytetrafluoroethylene, or PTFE. It is a synthetic polymer known for its slick, non-stick properties. For decades, PTFE coatings were common in cookware because they prevented food from sticking. This made cooking and cleaning much easier for everyone.

However, concerns grew about a chemical used in the past to make PTFE: Perfluorooctanoic acid, or PFOA. PFOA was a processing aid. While PFOA itself was mostly burned off during manufacturing, small amounts could remain. Research linked PFOA to potential health issues, leading to its phase-out from production. Today, PTFE-based products are generally made without PFOA. Even so, many people prefer to avoid PTFE altogether due to past concerns or worry about fumes from overheating. This is why brands like Gourmia focus on alternative materials. If you want to know more about Gourmia and Teflon, explore [is Gourmia air fryer made of Teflon](https://homeessentialsguide.com/is-gourmia-air-fryer-made-of-teflon).

### Health Considerations of Traditional Non-Stick

When traditional non-stick pans heat to very high temperatures, they can release fumes. These fumes are usually harmless with normal use. However, if a pan overheats excessively, above 500°F (260°C), the PTFE coating can break down. This breakdown releases tiny particles and gases. Breathing these fumes can cause temporary flu-like symptoms, sometimes called "Teflon flu." This is rare in home cooking, as air fryers typically operate below such extreme temperatures.

The bigger health concern historically revolved around PFOA. This chemical was linked to issues like kidney disease and high cholesterol in studies. Because of these findings, PFOA has been eliminated from most non-stick cookware manufacturing since 2013. Most modern PTFE products are PFOA-free. Yet, many consumers still seek out non-PTFE options for peace of mind. This preference has driven the growth of ceramic and other non-stick alternatives in the market. Knowing this helps clarify why manufacturers promote "Teflon-free" claims.

## Maintaining Your Gourmia Air Fryer's Non-Stick Coating

Proper maintenance is key to making any non-stick coating last a long time, regardless of whether it's Teflon-free or not. ### Best Practices for Longevity

To ensure your Gourmia air fryer's non-stick coating remains effective, follow these simple best practices:

*   **Use appropriate utensils:** Always use silicone, plastic, or wooden utensils when cooking in your air fryer. Metal utensils can easily scratch and damage the non-stick surface, leading to flaking.
*   **Avoid cooking sprays with propellants:** Some cooking sprays contain chemicals that can build up on non-stick surfaces, creating a sticky residue that is hard to remove and can degrade the coating. Instead, use a spray bottle with a little oil or brush oil directly onto your food.
*   **Store carefully:** When stacking air fryer baskets or other cookware, place a soft cloth or paper towel between them. This prevents scratches from contact.
*   **Hand wash recommended:** While some Gourmia baskets may be dishwasher safe, hand washing is often gentler and prolongs the life of the non-stick coating.
