### Can I use aluminum foil in my Ninja Dual Air Fryer?
Yes, you can generally use aluminum foil in your Ninja Dual Air Fryer. It is safe to line the baskets with foil for easier cleanup or to prevent sticking. However, avoid covering the entire bottom of the basket, as this can impede airflow and affect cooking performance. Always ensure the foil does not touch the heating element.
