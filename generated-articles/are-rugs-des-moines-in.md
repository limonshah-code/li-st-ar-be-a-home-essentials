### Daily Care for Your Rugs

Daily care is simple but effective. Vacuum your rugs regularly. How often depends on foot traffic. High-traffic areas need more frequent vacuuming. Use the right vacuum setting for your rug's pile height. Avoid using a beater bar on delicate rugs.

Rotate your rugs every few months. This ensures even wear. It also prevents sun fading in one area. If a spill happens, act fast. Blot the spill, do not rub it. Use a clean cloth and a gentle cleaning solution. For specific rug materials, like wool, understand their care requirements.
