## FAQs About Cascade Dishwasher Detergent

### Is Cascade bad for dishwashers?
No, Cascade is not bad for dishwashers when used as directed. Its formulas are designed to be safe for dishwasher components. Some people worry about residue or etching, but these issues usually stem from incorrect dosing, water hardness, or not using rinse aid. Using the right Cascade product for your water type and dishwasher model helps protect your machine.

### Can I use Cascade in my Bosch dishwasher?
Yes, you can use Cascade in your Bosch dishwasher. Bosch dishwashers are high-performance machines that benefit from quality detergents. Cascade Platinum is often recommended for Bosch models due to its strong cleaning power and anti-film properties. Always follow Bosch's recommendations for detergent type (e.g., pacs are generally preferred over powders).

### What is the difference between Cascade Platinum and Complete?
Cascade Platinum offers the highest level of cleaning, designed to tackle 24-hour stuck-on food, fight film, and provide a superior shine. It generally includes more powerful enzymes and degreasers. Cascade Complete provides strong everyday cleaning and grease removal, with a built-in rinse aid. It is a great balance of performance and value for less heavily soiled dishes.

### Do I need to pre-rinse dishes with Cascade?
For Cascade Platinum, pre-rinsing is unnecessary. It contains powerful enzymes that break down even dried-on food. With Cascade Complete, you can often skip pre-rinsing for most everyday messes. For Cascade Original or gel formulas, a quick scrape or light rinse might be beneficial, especially for very sticky or dried-on food, to achieve the best results.

### Where should I put Cascade detergent in my dishwasher?
For ActionPacs, simply place one pac in the main detergent dispenser cup. Do not open or puncture the pac. For Cascade powder or gel, fill the main detergent dispenser cup to the recommended line. Some dishwashers have a pre-wash compartment; for heavily soiled loads, you can put a small amount of powder or gel there, or use a pac designed for pre-wash.

### Is Cascade dishwasher detergent gluten-free?
Cascade dishwasher detergents are generally considered gluten-free. While they are not food products and not intended for consumption, Procter & Gamble (the maker of Cascade) typically formulates household cleaning products without gluten-containing ingredients. If you have extreme sensitivities, you might contact the manufacturer directly for the most up-to-date information regarding specific product formulations.
