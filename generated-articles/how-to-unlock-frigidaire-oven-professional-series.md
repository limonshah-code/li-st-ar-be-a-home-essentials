### FAQ Section

#### Q1: How long does a Frigidaire oven stay locked after self-clean?
A Frigidaire oven stays locked after a self-clean cycle until it cools down to a safe temperature. This usually takes 30 to 90 minutes after the cycle completes. The exact time depends on the oven's insulation and ambient room temperature. Do not attempt to open the door before it unlocks automatically.

#### Q2: Can I manually unlock a Frigidaire oven door?
Generally, you cannot manually unlock a Frigidaire oven door once the safety lock engages. The locking mechanism is electronic and designed to prevent manual override during high-temperature operations. Attempting to force the door open can damage the latch, control board, or the door itself. Always follow manufacturer-recommended procedures.

#### Q3: What if my Frigidaire oven display shows an error code?
If your Frigidaire oven display shows an error code, note the code down. Then, consult your oven's user manual. The manual lists specific error codes and their meanings. It often provides troubleshooting steps. If the issue persists after following manual instructions, contact a certified appliance technician for diagnosis and repair.

#### Q4: Is it safe to force open a locked oven door?
No, it is not safe to force open a locked oven door. Forcing the door can cause severe damage to the door latch mechanism, hinges, and electronic components. It can also be very dangerous if the oven is hot. Always wait for the oven to cool and try troubleshooting steps. If the door remains locked, seek professional help.

#### Q5: Why is my Frigidaire oven Professional Series door still locked after a power cycle?
If your Frigidaire Professional Series oven door remains locked after a power cycle, several reasons could apply. The control board might be faulty, or the door latch motor could be stuck or broken. #### Q6: How do I reset a Frigidaire Professional Series oven?
To reset a Frigidaire Professional Series oven, first press the "Cancel" or "Clear/Off" button on the control panel. If that does not work, perform a hard reset.
