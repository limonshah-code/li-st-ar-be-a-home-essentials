## Why Frigidaire Ovens Warn Against Foil Use

You just got a new Frigidaire oven, or maybe you are cleaning an older one. You open the door and see a clear warning label: "Do Not Use Foil." This warning might make you wonder why such a common kitchen item is prohibited. Many of us use aluminum foil for baking or for lining oven bottoms to catch spills. However, modern Frigidaire ovens, and most ovens, have specific reasons for this strict instruction. Ignoring this warning can lead to serious problems. These problems include oven damage, safety hazards, and even voiding your appliance warranty. This article explains why your Frigidaire oven says do not use foil. We discuss the risks involved and offer safe alternatives. I will guide you through understanding proper oven care for your appliance.
