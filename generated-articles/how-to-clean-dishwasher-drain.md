## How to Clean Your Dishwasher Drain for Optimal Performance

Is your dishwasher leaving dishes dirty? Does it smell bad after a cycle? A clogged dishwasher drain often causes these frustrating problems. Food particles, grease, and soap scum build up over time. This blockage stops proper draining and reduces cleaning power. Learning how to clean your dishwasher drain is simple. It brings back sparkling dishes and a fresh kitchen. This guide will show you easy, effective steps to clear your dishwasher drain. We will cover routine checks, common clogs, and powerful cleaning methods. You will soon have your dishwasher working perfectly again
