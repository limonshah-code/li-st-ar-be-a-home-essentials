## FAQ Section

### Is the Nama J2 Juicer good for celery?
Yes, the Nama J2 Juicer is excellent for celery. Its powerful motor and slow masticating action efficiently extract juice from fibrous vegetables. The large hopper allows you to add whole celery stalks or large pieces, reducing your need for pre-chopping. This makes juicing celery much faster and more convenient.

### Is the Hurom H200 a good slow juicer?
The Hurom H200 is a very good slow juicer. It uses Hurom's patented Slow Squeeze Technology to extract juice gently. This process minimizes oxidation and heat, preserving vital nutrients and enzymes. It produces high-quality, smooth juice with minimal foam, making it a top choice for health-conscious individuals.

### How often should you clean a Nama J2 Juicer?
You should clean your Nama J2 Juicer immediately after each use. This prevents pulp from drying and sticking to the parts, making cleanup much easier. A quick rinse under running water and a scrub with the provided brush are usually enough. Regular cleaning helps maintain hygiene and the juicer's performance.

### Can the Hurom H200 juice whole fruits?
The Hurom H200 can juice many whole fruits, thanks to its Mega Hopper. Small apples, oranges, or peeled citrus can often go in without much chopping. However, larger or harder fruits may still require some pre-cutting to ensure smooth operation and prevent clogging. Always refer to the user manual for best practices.

### What is the main difference between cold press and centrifugal juicers?
The main difference lies in how they extract juice. Cold press (masticating) juicers press and grind produce slowly, preserving nutrients and producing higher yield. Centrifugal juicers use fast-spinning blades and a mesh strainer, which generates heat and can cause oxidation, leading to less nutrient-dense juice and more foam.

### Do Nama J2 and Hurom H200 juicers make noise?
Both the Nama J2 and Hurom H200 are masticating juicers, which are significantly quieter than centrifugal juicers. They operate at low speeds, producing a gentle hum rather than a loud whirring sound. The Hurom H200 is often noted for being exceptionally quiet, making it suitable for early morning juicing sessions.
