### FAQs About Turning On Your GE Dishwasher

#### Why isn't my GE dishwasher turning on at all?
My GE dishwasher might not turn on due to a lack of power. First, check if the power cord is fully plugged into the outlet. Next, inspect your home's circuit breaker to see if it has tripped. Ensure any wall switch connected to the dishwasher is in the "on" position.

#### My GE dishwasher powers on, but the cycle won't start when I press "Start." What's wrong?
If your GE dishwasher powers on but won't start the cycle, the door might not be securely latched. Push the door firmly until you hear a click. Some models also require you to press "Start" within a few seconds of closing the door. Also, check for any error codes on the display.

#### Do I need to pre-rinse dishes before putting them in a GE dishwasher?
I generally recommend scraping large food particles off dishes, but extensive pre-rinsing is usually not necessary with modern GE dishwashers. Most models have sensors and powerful spray jets designed to handle typical food residues. Excessive pre-rinsing can even prevent smart sensors from detecting soil levels accurately.

#### How do I know which wash cycle to choose for my dishes?
Choosing the correct wash cycle depends on how dirty your dishes are. I use "Normal Wash" for everyday loads. "Heavy Wash" is best for pots and pans with stubborn food. "Light" or "Delicate" is for glassware and lightly soiled items. Refer to your GE owner's manual for specific cycle descriptions for your model.

#### How long does a typical GE dishwasher cycle take?
The duration of a GE dishwasher cycle varies based on the selected cycle and options. A "Normal Wash" might take 2-3 hours. "Heavy Wash" can be longer, while "Express" or "Quick Wash" cycles are usually much shorter, around 30-60 minutes. Options like "Sanitize" or "Heated Dry" also extend the total time.

#### My "Start" button is blinking, but the dishwasher isn't starting. What does this mean?
A blinking "Start" button often indicates that the dishwasher is paused, the door is not fully closed, or it's waiting for a command. Try opening and firmly re-closing the door.
