## Maintaining Your Insignia Washer for Longevity

Proper maintenance extends the life of any appliance. This holds true for your Insignia washer machine. Regular cleaning is crucial. Over time, detergent residue and mineral deposits build up. This affects performance. Use a washing machine cleaner monthly. You can also run a hot water cycle with vinegar or baking soda. This helps remove grime and odors.

Always use the correct amount of detergent. Too much detergent creates excess suds. This leaves residue on clothes and inside the machine. Too little detergent might not clean clothes effectively. Read your detergent instructions carefully. Match the amount to your load size and water hardness. This simple step protects your washer and clothes.

Inspect hoses regularly. Check for cracks or leaks. A small leak can cause significant water damage. Replace hoses if they show signs of wear. Ensure all connections are tight. This prevents unexpected problems. It is a quick check that saves money and stress. This simple task is often overlooked.

Do not overload your washing machine. Overloading stresses the motor and moving parts. It also prevents clothes from getting clean. Follow the manufacturer's guidelines for load capacity. Distribute clothes evenly in the drum. This balances the load. A balanced load reduces vibration and wear. These maintenance steps ensure your Insignia washer runs well for many years. It protects your investment and keeps your clothes fresh.
