## How To Clean Frigidaire Washing Machine Front Load

Have you ever opened your Frigidaire front load washing machine only to be met with a musty smell? It is a common problem for many owners. Front-load washers, while efficient, can trap moisture. This moisture creates a perfect environment for mold and mildew to grow.

Cleaning your Frigidaire washing machine regularly is essential. It prevents unpleasant odors from transferring to your clothes. It also helps your machine work better and last longer. This guide will walk you through the steps to deep clean your Frigidaire front loader. We will cover everything from the door gasket to the filter.

### Takeaway

*   Regularly clean your Frigidaire washer's door gasket to prevent mold and mildew buildup.
*   Deep clean the drum using a washer cleaner, vinegar, or baking soda on a hot cycle.
*   Do not forget to clean the detergent dispenser and the drain pump filter.
*   Leave the washer door ajar after use to allow the interior to dry completely.
*   Use only HE (High-Efficiency) detergent and the correct amount for your loads.

### How do you clean a Frigidaire front load washing machine?

You clean a Frigidaire front load washing machine by regularly wiping the door gasket, running cleaning cycles with white vinegar or a specialized cleaner, and routinely clearing the dispenser and drain pump filter. This helps remove mold, mildew, and detergent residue.

### Why Your Frigidaire Front Load Washer Needs Regular Cleaning

Your Frigidaire front load washing machine works hard to keep your clothes clean. Over time, however, it can become a breeding ground for problems. This is especially true for front-loading models. They often face specific issues that require attention.

One main reason for cleaning is mold and mildew buildup. The door gasket on front loaders creates a tight seal. This seal traps moisture inside the drum after a wash cycle. This damp, dark environment is perfect for mold to thrive. Mold can cause a musty smell in your laundry and even affect your health.

Another common issue is detergent and fabric softener residue. These products can leave a sticky film inside the drum and dispenser. This residue can trap dirt and contribute to odors. It can also reduce your machine's efficiency. Regular cleaning washes away these sticky deposits. It ensures your clothes get truly clean every time. Without proper maintenance, these issues can even shorten the lifespan of your Frigidaire washer. This is why staying on top of your machine's cleanliness matters.

### Essential Tools and Cleaning Agents for Your Frigidaire Washer

Before you start cleaning your Frigidaire front load washing machine, gather your supplies. Having everything ready makes the process much smoother. You likely have most of these items already in your home. These tools and agents are effective for tackling common washer issues.

First, you will need white distilled vinegar. Vinegar is a natural disinfectant and deodorizer. It breaks down mineral deposits and soap scum. It also helps kill mold and mildew spores. For tougher jobs, or if you prefer, you can use bleach. Bleach is a powerful sanitizer, but always use it carefully and never mix it with vinegar.

Baking soda is another great natural cleaner. It helps absorb odors and provides a gentle abrasive for scrubbing. You will also need several microfiber cloths. These cloths are great for wiping surfaces without scratching them. An old toothbrush or a small scrub brush will be handy for tight spaces. Finally, a spray bottle is useful for applying cleaning solutions. Having these items on hand ensures you are prepared for a thorough cleaning.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Cleaning the Door Gasket/Boot Seal

The door gasket, also called the boot seal, is a critical area to clean on your Frigidaire front load washer. This rubber seal around the door often collects water, lint, and detergent residue. This creates a prime spot for mold and mildew to grow. Cleaning it regularly prevents those unpleasant musty smells from spreading to your clothes.

To start, open your washer door wide. Carefully pull back the rubber folds of the gasket. You will likely find grime, lint, and possibly dark mold spots. Be thorough as you inspect all the creases. This area can hide a surprising amount of buildup.

#### Bleach Solution Method

For a strong clean, mix a solution of 1 part bleach to 1 part water in your spray bottle. Spray this solution directly onto the moldy or grimy areas of the gasket. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes to allow the bleach to work. Then, use a clean microfiber cloth to wipe away all the grime. You might need to scrub stubborn spots with an old toothbrush. Remember to rinse the cloth often in clean water.

#### Vinegar Solution Method

If you prefer a more natural approach, use undiluted white vinegar. Soak a microfiber cloth in white vinegar. Then, thoroughly wipe down the entire gasket, ensuring you get into all the folds. For tougher mold, you can tuck vinegar-soaked cloths into the folds and let them sit for 30 minutes to an hour. Afterward, remove the cloths and wipe the gasket clean. This method is gentle but effective.

After cleaning, it is vital to dry the gasket completely. Use a separate dry cloth to wipe down all the rubber folds. Ensuring the gasket is dry helps prevent new mold growth. Leaving the washer door slightly ajar after each use also helps air circulate. This allows the gasket to dry naturally between washes. ### Deep Cleaning the Frigidaire Washer Drum and Interior

Cleaning the drum and interior of your Frigidaire front load washing machine is essential. This step removes built-up detergent residue, mineral deposits, and any lingering odors. A clean drum ensures your clothes come out fresh and truly clean. You have a few effective options for this deep cleaning process.

#### Cleaning with Washer Cleaner Tablets

Many people find specialized washer cleaner tablets convenient and effective. Brands like Affresh or Tide Washing Machine Cleaner work very well. You simply place one tablet directly into the washer drum. Do not put it in the detergent dispenser. Then, close the door and select the "Clean Washer" cycle if your Frigidaire machine has one. If not, choose the hottest water setting and the heaviest duty cycle. Run the cycle through to completion. These tablets are designed to dissolve and scrub away buildup.

#### Vinegar and Baking Soda Method

For a natural and budget-friendly alternative, use white vinegar and baking soda. First, pour two cups of white distilled vinegar directly into the detergent dispenser. Then, sprinkle a half cup of baking soda into the washer drum. Close the door and select the hottest water setting. Choose the longest or heaviest duty cycle available on your Frigidaire. Let the cycle run.

The vinegar works as a disinfectant and dissolves mineral deposits. The baking soda helps absorb odors and provides a gentle abrasive action. After the cycle finishes, wipe down the inside of the drum with a clean cloth. Make sure there is no residue left. This combination is powerful for eliminating smells and grime. It leaves your washer sparkling clean. These deep cleaning methods are important for all front load washers, regardless of brand. Remember to always use the hottest water setting for the best results.

### Cleaning the Detergent Dispenser and Fabric Softener Tray

The detergent dispenser and fabric softener tray on your Frigidaire front load washing machine can accumulate significant residue. Soap scum, softener buildup, and even mold can thrive in these areas. This buildup can clog the dispenser. It can also prevent your detergents from properly dispensing into the wash. This means your clothes might not get as clean as they should.

Most Frigidaire dispenser drawers are removable. First, carefully pull the drawer out as far as it goes. Look for a release tab or button, often located in the middle or on the side. Press or lift this tab and pull the drawer completely out. This allows you to access all parts of the dispenser.

Once the dispenser is out, you will likely see a sticky, slimy mess. Soak the entire drawer in a sink filled with hot water. You can add a cup of white vinegar or a little dish soap to the water. Let it soak for at least 30 minutes. This helps loosen the grime. After soaking, use an old toothbrush or a small scrub brush to thoroughly clean all compartments. Pay attention to the small corners and crevices where residue can hide. Rinse the drawer under running water until it is completely clean.

Do not forget the housing where the dispenser sits. Use a damp cloth or a spray bottle with a vinegar solution. Wipe down the inside of the dispenser housing. Reach into all the nooks and crannies you can access. Make sure to remove any visible mold or residue. Once both the drawer and housing are clean and dry, carefully reinsert the dispenser drawer. This simple step makes a big difference in your washer's performance.

### Locating and Cleaning the Drain Pump Filter (Coin Trap)

Many people overlook the drain pump filter, also known as the coin trap, in their Frigidaire front load washing machine. This filter is a crucial component. It catches lint, coins, buttons, and other small items that accidentally fall out of pockets. If this filter becomes clogged, it can prevent your washer from draining properly. This leads to standing water, unpleasant odors, and even error codes. Regular cleaning of this filter is a must.

The drain pump filter on Frigidaire front load washers is usually located at the bottom front of the machine. It is often behind a small access panel. You might need a flathead screwdriver or a coin to pry open this panel. Before opening, place a shallow pan or old towels on the floor beneath the filter. There will be residual water inside the hose and filter housing. This water will drain out when you open it.

Once the panel is open, you will see a small, usually short, drain hose and the filter cap. First, carefully remove the small drain hose from its clip. Place the end of the hose into your pan to drain any remaining water. Once the water stops flowing, secure the hose back in its clip. Next, slowly twist the large filter cap counter-clockwise to remove it. Be prepared for a little more water to spill out.

Once the filter is out, remove any lint, hair, coins, or other debris you find. Rinse the filter thoroughly under running water. Use an old toothbrush if needed to scrub away any stubborn gunk. Also, use a damp cloth to wipe out the filter housing inside the machine. Make sure it is clean. Once both the filter and housing are clean, screw the filter cap back on clockwise until it is tight. Close the access panel. Cleaning this filter makes a huge difference in your washer's efficiency and smell. ### Exterior Care and Preventing Future Odors in Your Frigidaire Washer

After thoroughly cleaning the interior components of your Frigidaire front load washing machine, it is time to address the exterior. While the exterior does not directly affect washing performance, keeping it clean helps maintain its appearance. It also prevents grime buildup around controls and surfaces. Use a soft, damp cloth and a mild all-purpose cleaner to wipe down the entire exterior. Pay attention to the control panel, knobs, and the top surface. Do not spray cleaner directly onto the control panel. Instead, spray it onto your cloth first. This prevents moisture from getting into the electronics.

Preventing future odors is crucial for a continuously fresh washer. The most important step is to always leave the washer door ajar after each wash cycle. This allows air to circulate inside the drum. It helps the interior dry completely. This simple action significantly reduces the conditions where mold and mildew thrive. It prevents that common musty smell.

Another key prevention tip involves your detergent. Always use High-Efficiency (HE) detergent in your Frigidaire front load washer. HE detergents produce less suds. Too many suds can leave behind excess residue, which contributes to odor and buildup. Also, use the correct amount of detergent. Many people use too much, which leads to residue buildup. Follow the manufacturer's recommendations on the detergent packaging. If your clothes are not heavily soiled, you might even be able to use less than recommended.

Finally, remove clothes from the washer promptly after the cycle finishes. Do not let wet laundry sit in the drum for extended periods. This can lead to mildew on your clothes and inside the machine. Incorporating these simple habits into your laundry routine will keep your Frigidaire washer fresh and odor-free. ### FAQ Section

#### How often should I clean my Frigidaire front load washer?
You should deep clean your Frigidaire front load washer at least once a month. This includes cleaning the drum and detergent dispenser. The door gasket should be wiped down after every few washes, or at least weekly. Regularly cleaning the drain pump filter every few months helps prevent clogs and odors.

#### Can I use bleach to clean my Frigidaire washer?
Yes, you can use bleach to clean your Frigidaire washer. It is very effective for killing mold and mildew. Use a bleach solution to wipe down the door gasket. For the drum, you can run a clean cycle with about 1/2 cup of bleach poured directly into the dispenser. Remember to never mix bleach with vinegar.

#### What causes mold in a front load washer?
Mold grows in a front load washer due to trapped moisture. The rubber door gasket creates a tight seal. This seal prevents air from circulating inside the drum. Water droplets and humidity remain, creating a damp and dark environment perfect for mold and mildew to thrive.

#### Why does my Frigidaire washer smell bad after cleaning?
If your Frigidaire washer still smells bad after cleaning, you might have missed a spot. The drain pump filter is a common culprit for lingering odors. Also, ensure the door gasket is thoroughly dry after cleaning and after each use. Sometimes, using too much detergent can also leave behind residue that causes a smell over time.

#### Is it okay to leave the washer door open?
Yes, it is highly recommended to leave the washer door slightly ajar after each use. This allows air to circulate inside the drum and helps the interior dry completely. This simple habit is one of the most effective ways to prevent mold, mildew, and unpleasant odors from developing in your front load washer.

#### Can I use regular detergent in my Frigidaire HE washer?
No, you should only use High-Efficiency (HE) detergent in your Frigidaire HE front load washer. Regular detergents produce too many suds. This excess foam can lead to residue buildup inside the machine. It can also clog components and contribute to foul odors. Always look for the "HE" symbol on your detergent bottle.
