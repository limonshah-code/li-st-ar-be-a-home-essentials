## Optimize Your Home: MERV 13 Filter Replacement Guide

Do you wonder how often to replace your MERV 13 filter? It is a common question. A MERV 13 filter plays a vital role in your home's air quality. It traps tiny particles, like allergens and dust, keeping your air cleaner. Replacing this filter on time is not just about clean air; it also impacts your HVAC system's health and energy use.
