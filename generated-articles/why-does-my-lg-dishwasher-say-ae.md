## Decoding the "AE" Error: Why Does My LG Dishwasher Say AE?

Discovering an error code on your home appliance can cause immediate concern. When your LG dishwasher says "AE", it signals a specific internal issue. This code indicates that the dishwasher has detected water where it should not be. It is a safety measure to prevent potential water damage in your home.
