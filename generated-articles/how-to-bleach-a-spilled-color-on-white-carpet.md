## Restore Your White Carpet: Bleaching Spilled Colors Away

A vibrant color spill on your white carpet can cause immediate worry. It often feels like a permanent disaster. Bringing your carpet back to its original bright, clean state seems challenging. This article gives you clear instructions on how to bleach a spilled color on white carpet safely. You will learn effective methods for removing tough color stains. We cover essential steps, safety measures, and alternative solutions. Get ready to tackle common color spills with confidence and restore your carpet's beauty.

**Takeaway:**
*   Always test bleach solutions in an inconspicuous area first.
*   Use diluted bleach or oxygen bleach for best results.
