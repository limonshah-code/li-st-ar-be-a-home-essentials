## Preparing Your Hey Dudes for the Washing Machine: Step-by-Step

Proper preparation is essential before you machine wash Hey Dude shoes. Skipping these steps can lead to damaged shoes or even a damaged washing machine. I always recommend taking your time with this stage. It ensures a safer and more effective cleaning process.

First, remove any loose dirt or debris from your shoes. You can use a soft brush or a dry cloth for this. Pay attention to the soles. Dirt and small pebbles often get stuck in the treads. Knocking off this excess dirt prevents it from clogging your washing machine. It also keeps your machine cleaner. A quick pre-clean makes the machine washing more efficient.

Next, take out the insoles. Hey Dude insoles are typically made of memory foam or cork. These materials can absorb a lot of water. They also take a long time to dry. Machine washing insoles can damage them. They might lose their shape or cushioning. I recommend hand washing insoles separately with mild soap and water. Let them air dry completely before putting them back in your shoes. This step also allows for a more thorough cleaning of the shoe's interior. For tips on cleaning the inside of shoes, read about [how to clean the inside of shoes](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-the-inside-of-shoes).

Then, remove the laces. Hey Dude laces are usually simple elastic cords or traditional laces. They can tangle around other items in the wash. Laces might also get caught in the washing machine agitator. This could damage both the laces and your machine. You can hand wash laces or put them in a small mesh bag with the shoes. Place your now de-laced and de-insoled shoes into a large mesh laundry bag. This bag protects the shoes from the machine's drum. It also keeps them from banging around. This prevents friction and preserves the shoe's shape. It is a critical step for shoe longevity.

## Washing Cycle and Detergent Choices for Hey Dudes

Selecting the correct washing machine settings and detergent is vital. It directly impacts the safety and effectiveness of cleaning your Hey Dude shoes. Using the wrong settings can damage the shoes. It can also cause them to lose their color or shape. I always stress caution at this stage.

Set your washing machine to a gentle or delicate cycle. This is crucial for washing Hey Dude shoes. A gentle cycle uses less agitation. It mimics hand washing. This reduces the stress on the fabric and seams of your shoes. Avoid any heavy-duty or normal cycles. These are too aggressive for most footwear. Also, choose cold water. Cold water prevents shrinking and color bleeding. Hot water can also damage the shoe's adhesive. This can make them fall apart faster. Some washing machines have specific settings for shoes or bulky items. If your machine has such a setting, like an [LG washing machine](https://homeessentialsguide.com/can-you-wash-shoes-in-lg-washing-machine), consider using it.

For detergent, use a small amount of mild liquid laundry detergent. Avoid powdered detergents. They might not dissolve completely in cold water. This can leave residue on your shoes. Do not use bleach or harsh chemicals. Bleach can discolor your shoes. It can also weaken the fabric fibers. Using too much detergent can also leave a soapy film. This film can attract more dirt later.

I often add a few old towels to the wash cycle. These towels provide cushioning. They prevent the shoes from banging against the drum. This reduces noise and protects both the shoes and the machine. Make sure the towels are colorfast. You do not want their dyes to transfer to your shoes. Once the cycle finishes, take your Hey Dudes out immediately. Do not leave them sitting in the wet machine. This prevents mildew growth.

## Drying Your Hey Dude Shoes: The Crucial Final Step

Drying your Hey Dude shoes properly is as important as washing them. Incorrect drying methods can cause irreparable damage. They can shrink the shoes. They can also warp their shape or damage the materials. I always emphasize air drying. It is the safest and most effective method.

Never put Hey Dude shoes in a tumble dryer. The high heat from a dryer can cause the shoe's fabric to shrink. It can also melt the adhesive that holds the sole. This leads to the shoe falling apart. The elastic in some Hey Dude styles can also degrade with heat. This affects the shoe's comfortable fit. Always avoid direct heat sources.

After washing, remove your shoes from the mesh bag. Gently reshape them by hand. You can stuff them with paper towels or old newspapers. This helps absorb moisture. It also helps maintain the shoe's original shape as it dries. Avoid using colored newspaper. The ink might transfer to your shoes. Replace the paper when it becomes damp.

Place your Hey Dudes in a well-ventilated area. A spot with good airflow is ideal. This could be outdoors in a shady spot or indoors near a fan. Avoid direct sunlight. Intense sun can fade the colors of your shoes. It can also make some materials brittle. Drying can take anywhere from 12 to 48 hours. This depends on humidity and airflow. Be patient. Ensure they are completely dry before you wear them or put the insoles back. Wearing damp shoes can lead to odor and mildew. It also feels uncomfortable. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I put Hey Dudes in the dryer?
No, you should never put Hey Dude shoes in a dryer. The high heat can shrink the fabric, melt the glue, and damage the sole material. Always air dry your Hey Dude shoes by placing them in a well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight or heat sources.

### How often should I wash my Hey Dude shoes?
The frequency of washing Hey Dude shoes depends on how often you wear them and how dirty they get. For light use, spot cleaning is often enough. For regular wear or visible dirt, a full wash every few months might be appropriate. Avoid overwashing to extend their lifespan.

### What kind of detergent is safe for Hey Dudes?
Use a small amount of mild liquid laundry detergent. Avoid harsh chemicals, bleach, or fabric softeners. Powdered detergents can leave residue, so liquid is preferred. A gentle, eco-friendly detergent is often a good choice to protect the shoe's fabric and color.

### Can I machine wash Hey Dude insoles?
It is not recommended to machine wash Hey Dude insoles. They are often made of memory foam or cork, which can be damaged by machine agitation and excessive water. Hand wash insoles separately with mild soap and water, then air dry them completely.

### Will my Hey Dudes shrink if I machine wash them?
There is a risk of shrinking if you use hot water or a high heat dryer. To minimize shrinkage, always wash Hey Dude shoes in cold water on a gentle cycle. Always air dry them afterward. Following these steps helps maintain their original size and shape.

### How do I remove stubborn stains from Hey Dude shoes?
For stubborn stains, try spot cleaning with a paste made from baking soda and a little water. Apply the paste to the stain, let it sit for a bit, then gently scrub with a soft brush.
