## Finding the Perfect Rug Size for Your 90-Inch Table

A rug completes your dining room. It defines the space, adds warmth, and absorbs sound. Choosing the correct size, especially for a substantial 90-inch table, prevents a common design mistake. A rug that is too small makes your dining area feel cramped and unfinished. I want to help you avoid this.

This guide helps you understand the rules for rug sizing. We will look at chair clearance, room dimensions, and common mistakes. You will learn exactly **what size rug for 90 inch table** works best. I will give you clear steps to measure your space. You will select a rug that looks good and functions well. Let's make your dining room inviting and stylish.

### Takeaway

*   **Measure Your Room First:** Know your room's length and width. This sets the boundary for your rug choice.
*   **Allow for Chair Movement:** Ensure all chair legs stay on the rug, even when pulled out. This usually means adding 24-30 inches to each side of your table's dimensions.
*   **Standard Recommendations:** For a 90-inch rectangular table, a 9x12 foot or 10x14 foot rug often works well.
