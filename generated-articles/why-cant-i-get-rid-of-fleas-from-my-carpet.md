## Persistent Fleas: Why Your Carpet Keeps Them

Imagine the scene: you've vacuumed relentlessly, treated your pets, and yet those tiny, biting pests still leap from your carpet. It's an infuriating experience, making you wonder, "Why can't I get rid of fleas from my carpet?" You are not alone in this battle. Many homeowners face ongoing struggles with flea infestations despite their best efforts.

The persistent nature of fleas often stems from a misunderstanding of their life cycle and the places they hide. We will explore the reasons behind stubborn carpet flea problems. This guide offers practical solutions to help you reclaim your home. We will cover everything from the flea life cycle to effective treatment methods and important prevention tips.
