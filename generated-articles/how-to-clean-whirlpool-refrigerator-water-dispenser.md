## Achieve Pure Water: How To Clean Your Whirlpool Refrigerator Water Dispenser

Your Whirlpool refrigerator water dispenser offers convenience. It provides instant access to cool, filtered water. However, this dispenser needs regular cleaning to work well and provide healthy water. Water and cool, dark spaces create conditions for mold and mineral buildup. ### Takeaway

*   Regularly clean your Whirlpool water dispenser to prevent mold and mineral buildup.
*   Disassemble and clean the drip tray, dispenser nozzle, and water outlet.
*   Use a solution of warm water and mild dish soap, or a vinegar solution, for cleaning.
*   Replace your water filter as recommended by Whirlpool.
*   Perform routine maintenance to ensure pure, fresh water always.

### How do I clean my Whirlpool refrigerator water dispenser?

You clean your Whirlpool refrigerator water dispenser by first turning off the water supply. Then, remove and wash the drip tray. Use a small brush or cotton swab with a mild soap solution to scrub the nozzle and internal parts. Rinse all components well and wipe down the exterior.
