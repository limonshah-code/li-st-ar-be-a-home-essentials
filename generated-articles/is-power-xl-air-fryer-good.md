#### Tips for Optimal Cooking Results

To achieve the best results with your Power XL Air Fryer, follow a few simple tips. First, do not overcrowd the basket. Overfilling prevents hot air from circulating properly. This leads to soggy or unevenly cooked food. Cook in batches if necessary. Second, shake the basket or flip food halfway through cooking. This ensures all sides are exposed to the hot air. This promotes even crisping and browning.

Third, use a little oil, even if the recipe says none. A light spray of oil or tossing with a teaspoon of oil can significantly improve crispiness and flavor, especially for fresh foods. Fourth, preheat your air fryer when the recipe calls for it. Preheating helps food cook more evenly and prevents it from sticking. This also allows the appliance to reach its optimal temperature before food goes in. These simple steps can elevate your air frying game.
