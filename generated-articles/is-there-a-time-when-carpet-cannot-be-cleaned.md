## When Carpet Cannot Be Cleaned: Knowing the Tipping Point

You love your carpet. It makes your home cozy and comfortable. Regular cleaning keeps it looking good and feeling fresh. But you might wonder, "Is there a time when carpet cannot be cleaned?" I ask myself this question too, especially when facing tough stains or older carpets. While most carpets respond well to professional cleaning, some situations go beyond repair. We will explore these specific scenarios today. This article will help you understand when to call a professional or consider replacement for your carpet.

### Takeaway:

*   **Assess Damage Clearly:** Look for mold, heavy water damage, or structural breakdown before deciding on cleaning.
*   **Identify Contamination:** Biohazards, toxic spills, or very deep-set odors often point towards replacement.
*   **Consider Age and Wear:** Extremely old or heavily worn carpets may not benefit from cleaning efforts.
*   **Seek Expert Advice:** A professional carpet cleaner can evaluate severe damage and guide your decision.
*   **Protect Your Health:** Replace carpets that pose health risks from mold, allergens, or dangerous contaminants.
