## Installing Carpet in Your New Construction Home

Building a new home is an exciting journey. You get to choose every detail, including the flooring. If you plan to install carpet in new construction, you are in the right place. Many homeowners find carpet provides comfort and warmth. It also improves sound insulation in a new build. This guide will walk you through each step of the process. We cover everything from initial planning to the final touches. Get ready to give your new home the cozy feel you desire.

### Takeaway

Installing carpet in new construction requires careful preparation and precise execution.
*   Ensure the subfloor is clean and flat.
*   Properly install tack strips and a quality carpet pad.
*   Use specific tools like knee kickers and power stretchers for a tight fit.
*   Accurate measurements and careful seaming lead to a professional finish.
*   Follow post-installation care for long-lasting enjoyment.

Carpet installation in new construction involves preparing a clean subfloor, installing tack strips and padding, precisely cutting and seaming carpet sections, and stretching the material for a tight, professional finish. This process creates a comfortable, durable floor covering from the ground up.
