## How To Clean An Iron On The Inside for Peak Performance

Have you ever noticed your iron spitting brown flakes onto your freshly laundered clothes? Or maybe the steam output feels weak, making ironing a chore? These common issues often point to one culprit: mineral buildup inside your iron. Knowing how to clean an iron on the inside is not just about keeping your appliance working; it is about protecting your clothes from unsightly stains and ensuring smooth, efficient ironing every time. This comprehensive guide will walk you through the simple steps to restore your iron's internal cleanliness and performance.
