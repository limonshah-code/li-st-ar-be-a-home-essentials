## Decoding the GE Serial Number for the Manufacturing Year

Once you have your GE dishwasher's serial number, you can decode it to find the manufacturing year. GE uses a specific system where the first two letters of the serial number represent the month and year of manufacture. This system makes it easy to pinpoint the exact time your appliance was made. The first letter corresponds to the year, and the second letter indicates the month.

GE's year codes repeat in a 12-year cycle. You will need to consider the age of your dishwasher to determine the correct cycle. For example, a "V" might represent 2011 or 2023. If your dishwasher looks relatively new, you can assume it is the most recent year in the cycle. If it appears much older, you might pick an earlier year.

Here is a typical GE serial number year code chart:

*   A = 1993, 2005, 2017
*   D = 1994, 2006, 2018
*   F = 1995, 2007, 2019
*   G = 1996, 2008, 2020
*   H = 1997, 2009, 2021
*   L = 1998, 2010, 2022
*   M = 1999, 2011, 2023
*   R = 2000, 2012, 2024
*   S = 2001, 2013, 2025
*   T = 2002, 2014, 2026
*   V = 2003, 2015, 2027
*   Z = 2004, 2016, 2028

The month code follows a standard alphabetical order. A=January, B=February, C=March, and so on. This consistent system applies across many GE appliances, not just dishwashers. ## Understanding the GE Date Code System

The GE date code system relies on an alphabetical sequence to denote manufacturing dates. This system is straightforward once you know the pattern. As discussed, the first letter represents the year, and the second letter stands for the month. This two-letter prefix makes up the core of the date code. The rest of the serial number contains other manufacturing details specific to the product run.

Let's look at an example. If your GE dishwasher serial number begins with "ML," the "M" is the year code, and the "L" is the month code. Referring to our year chart, "M" could be 1999, 2011, or 2023. Given that "L" corresponds to December (the 12th letter), the dishwasher would have been manufactured in December of 1999, 2011, or 2023. You would use the appliance's overall condition and purchase date to decide the most likely year.

This coding method helps maintain consistency across GE's product lines. It helps service technicians and parts suppliers quickly identify the correct version of an appliance. ## FAQ Section

### Q1: Where is the serial number located on a GE dishwasher?

The serial number on a GE dishwasher is typically found on a rating plate. This plate is usually located on the inner rim of the dishwasher door. You might see it on the side, top, or bottom edges when the door is open. Sometimes it is on the side of the tub or behind the kick plate.

### Q2: How do GE serial numbers indicate the year?

GE serial numbers use a two-letter code at the beginning to indicate the manufacturing date. The first letter represents the year, and the second letter represents the month. GE year codes repeat every 12 years. You use a reference chart to decode these letters into specific years and months.

### Q3: Can I find the age without the serial number?

Yes, you can find the age without the serial number. Check your original purchase receipts or the owner's manual. These documents often list the purchase date or manufacturing date.
