#### What Qualifies as a Defective Mattress?

A defective mattress has a manufacturing flaw. This could include issues like:

*   **Sagging:** Significant indentations (over 1.5 inches) that appear prematurely. This is not normal body impression.
*   **Coil Issues:** Springs that are poking through or making excessive noise in innerspring models.
*   **Material Breakdown:** Foam that breaks down or crumbles unexpectedly.
*   **Cover Defects:** Rips or tears in the mattress cover that were present upon arrival.

These issues are typically covered by Zinus's warranty. The warranty is separate from the 100-night trial. It protects against manufacturing defects. If you receive a mattress with a defect, do not wait. Contact Zinus immediately.

Damage that occurs during shipping is also not your fault. This could include:

*   **Dirt or Stains:** The mattress arrives soiled despite being new.

If your Zinus mattress arrives damaged, document it. Take clear photos or videos before unboxing. Also, note any damage on the delivery receipt if possible. This evidence is crucial. It helps Zinus process a claim with the shipping company. Report shipping damage right away, ideally within 24-48 hours of delivery.
