## What to Look for When Choosing an Area Rug

## Maintaining Your Area Rugs for Long-Term Beauty

Once you buy an area rug, proper care is important. Regular cleaning extends its lifespan and keeps it looking fresh. Vacuum your rug regularly to remove dirt and dust. For spills, act quickly. Blot the spill with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, as this can spread the stain.

Different rug materials require specific cleaning methods. Wool rugs might need professional cleaning periodically. Synthetic rugs are often easier to clean at home.
