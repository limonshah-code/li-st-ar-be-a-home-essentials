## My Dishwasher Is Not Turning On: Troubleshooting Power Issues

You have found the power button, but your Fisher & Paykel dishwasher still will not turn on. This can be frustrating. Several common issues can prevent your appliance from starting. First, check the power supply. Make sure the dishwasher is correctly plugged into a working outlet. A loose plug is a common culprit.

Next, check your home's circuit breaker. A tripped breaker will cut power to the dishwasher. Locate your electrical panel. Find the breaker labeled for your kitchen or dishwasher. Flip it off, then back on. This often restores power. Sometimes, a power surge can trip a breaker.

Ensure the dishwasher door is fully latched. Many dishwashers have a safety mechanism. They will not start if the door is not completely closed. Listen for a click when you shut the door. ## FAQ Section

### Q1: Where is the ON/OFF switch on a Fisher & Paykel DishDrawer?
A1: On Fisher & Paykel DishDrawer models, the ON/OFF switch is usually located on the top edge of the drawer itself. You need to pull the drawer open slightly to access the control panel strip on the inner top rim. Look for a circular button or a symbol indicating power.

### Q2: How do I turn on my integrated Fisher & Paykel dishwasher?
A2: To turn on an integrated Fisher & Paykel dishwasher, you must first open the appliance door. The control panel, which includes the power button, is typically found along the top edge of the door. Press the clearly marked power button to activate the dishwasher.

### Q3: Why is my Fisher & Paykel dishwasher not responding when I press the power button?
A3: If your Fisher & Paykel dishwasher does not respond, check the power supply. Ensure the plug is secure in the outlet and the circuit breaker has not tripped. Also, make sure the dishwasher door is fully closed and latched. A safety mechanism prevents operation if the door is open.

### Q4: Do all Fisher & Paykel dishwashers have the same power button location?
A4: No, the power button location varies among Fisher & Paykel dishwasher models. DishDrawer, integrated, and traditional designs each have different placements. Always refer to your specific model's user manual for the exact location and operating instructions.

### Q5: Can a power surge affect my dishwasher's ability to turn on?
A5: Yes, a power surge can affect your dishwasher's ability to turn on. It can cause a circuit breaker to trip, cutting power to the appliance. In some cases, a severe surge might damage internal components, leading to a permanent failure to power on.

### Q6: What should I do if my Fisher & Paykel dishwasher's power button is broken?
A6: If your Fisher & Paykel dishwasher's power button is physically broken or unresponsive, do not attempt to repair it yourself. Disconnect the power to the appliance and contact Fisher & Paykel customer support or a qualified appliance technician. They can diagnose and safely fix the issue.
