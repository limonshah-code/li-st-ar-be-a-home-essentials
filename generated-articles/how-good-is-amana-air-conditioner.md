### Understanding SEER and SEER2 Ratings

SEER is a measure of an air conditioner's efficiency. A higher SEER number means more efficient cooling. For example, a 16 SEER unit uses less energy than a 13 SEER unit. Amana offers many high-SEER options. These options meet or exceed current energy standards. New standards, called SEER2, took effect in 2023. SEER2 ratings are slightly different but still measure efficiency. Amana has adapted its product line to these new standards.

I always recommend checking the SEER or SEER2 rating when buying. It directly impacts your operating costs. Units with higher ratings may cost more upfront.
