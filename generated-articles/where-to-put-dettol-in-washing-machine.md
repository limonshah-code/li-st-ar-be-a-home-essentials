## Unlock Laundry Hygiene: Where To Put Dettol In Your Washing Machine

### Takeaway

*   **Main Wash Drawer:** Add Dettol Laundry Cleanser to the main wash detergent compartment with your regular detergent for general disinfection.
*   **Fabric Softener Compartment:** Use Dettol Laundry Cleanser here during the final rinse cycle for fresh, sanitized clothes.
*   **Direct to Drum:** Pour Dettol Laundry Cleanser directly into the drum before adding clothes for heavily soiled or germ-prone items.
*   **Pre-Soak Solution:** Create a diluted Dettol solution for pre-soaking stubborn items before machine washing.
*   **Washing Machine Clean:** Run an empty hot wash with Dettol in the detergent drawer to sanitize your machine itself.

### How much Dettol should I use per wash?

### Can Dettol replace my regular laundry detergent?
No, Dettol Laundry Cleanser cannot replace your regular laundry detergent. Dettol is a sanitizing additive. It kills bacteria and eliminates odors. Laundry detergent is essential for cleaning dirt, grease, and stains from clothes. Dettol works best when used in conjunction with your usual detergent.
