### Why Top Rack is Best for Your Nalgene

You should always place your Nalgene bottle on the top rack of your dishwasher. This is a simple but critical rule. The top rack is generally farther from the heating element. It experiences less intense heat during the wash cycle.

The bottom rack often gets much hotter water. This high heat can cause plastic items to warp or melt. While Tritan plastic is heat-resistant, extreme temperatures can still damage it. Placing your Nalgene bottle on the top rack protects its shape. It ensures your bottle lasts longer.

The water jets on the top rack are also less powerful. This helps prevent scratches or other wear on your bottle. Keeping it on the top rack maintains its clear finish. It keeps your Nalgene looking new for many uses. This simple step helps maintain your bottle's integrity.

### Avoiding Plastic Warping and Damage

Plastic warping is a common fear when using a dishwasher. This happens when plastic gets too hot. The material softens and loses its original shape. For your Nalgene, this can mean a bottle that no longer seals properly. It might even make it unusable.

To avoid warping, always place your Nalgene on the top rack. This part of the dishwasher is furthest from the heating element. It receives less intense heat. Also, choose a cycle that does not use extremely high temperatures. A standard wash cycle is usually sufficient for cleaning.

Another tip is to let your Nalgene air dry if possible. Some dishwashers have very hot drying cycles. This extra heat can also contribute to warping. Opening the dishwasher door after the wash cycle lets the bottle cool gradually. This simple step helps keep your Nalgene in perfect shape.

## FAQ Section

### Q1: Is Nalgene BPA-free and safe in the dishwasher?
Yes, modern Nalgene bottles are made from Tritan plastic. This material is 100% BPA-free. It is designed to be dishwasher safe. You can wash your Nalgene without concerns about BPA leaching.

### Q2: Can I wash Nalgene caps and lids in the dishwasher?
Yes, Nalgene caps and lids are also dishwasher safe. Place them on the top rack of your dishwasher. This prevents warping from high heat. Make sure they are positioned so water can reach all surfaces for a thorough clean.

### Q3: What temperature is safe for Nalgene in the dishwasher?
Nalgene bottles are safe for typical dishwasher temperatures. They handle cycles up to about 120-130°F (49-54°C) well. Avoid very high-temperature settings, like sanitize cycles. Always use the top rack to minimize heat exposure.

### Q4: How do I remove cloudy residue from my Nalgene after washing?
Cloudy residue often results from mineral deposits in hard water. Try handwashing with white vinegar. Fill the bottle with a 50/50 mixture of water and vinegar. Let it sit for several hours, then scrub with a brush and rinse well.

### Q5: Can old Nalgene bottles go in the dishwasher?
Older Nalgene bottles made of polycarbonate plastic were also typically dishwasher safe. However, Nalgene switched to BPA-free Tritan due to health concerns. If your bottle is very old or shows signs of wear, handwashing might be a gentler option. Always check for specific care instructions or signs of damage.
