## Control Lock vs. Other Dishwasher Features

It is important to distinguish the Control Lock from other dishwasher functions. Some users might confuse it with "Pause" or "Cancel" options. However, these features serve very different purposes. The Control Lock's main job is to disable the entire control panel. It prevents any button from working. It is a security feature.

The "Pause" button, in contrast, temporarily stops a running wash cycle. You use it to add a forgotten item. Or, you might need to briefly open the door. After pausing, you press "Start" to resume the cycle. The control panel remains active during a pause. You can still select different options, though they may not take effect until the cycle resumes. This is different from the lock.

The "Cancel" button, or "Drain" button on some models, ends a cycle completely. It often drains any water from the tub. You use it when you want to stop the wash process. The control panel is fully active when you press "Cancel." This allows you to choose a new cycle afterwards. The Control Lock, however, disables all these buttons. It makes the panel completely unresponsive. Understanding these differences helps you use your Kitchenaid dishwasher effectively. Each feature has a distinct role.

### Feature Distinctions

*   **Control Lock:** Disables all control panel buttons. Prevents any interaction with the machine. Primarily for safety and accidental presses.
*   **Pause:** Temporarily stops a running cycle. Allows opening the door to add items. The panel remains active.
*   **Cancel/Drain:** Stops a running cycle completely. Drains water from the tub. The panel remains active for new selections.
*   **Delay Start:** Postpones the start of a wash cycle to a later time. The panel is active for setting the delay.

The Control Lock ensures that once you set your dishwasher, it runs without interruption. Other buttons manage the cycle's progress directly. This clear distinction helps maintain efficiency and safety in your kitchen.

### What does "LOC" mean on my Kitchenaid dishwasher display?
