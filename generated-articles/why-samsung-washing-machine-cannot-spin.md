## Overloading and Unbalanced Loads Preventing Samsung Washer Spin

One of the most frequent reasons a Samsung washing machine cannot spin is an unbalanced or overloaded drum. Washing machines have sensors. These sensors detect if the load is evenly distributed. If clothes gather on one side, the machine will not spin. This prevents damage to the drum or motor. An overloaded machine also struggles to reach spin speed. The sheer weight becomes too much for the motor to handle efficiently.

When the machine senses an imbalance, it tries to redistribute the load. You might hear the machine stop, agitate a bit, and then try to spin again.
