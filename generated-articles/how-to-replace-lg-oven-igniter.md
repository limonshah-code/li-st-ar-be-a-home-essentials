## Replace Your LG Oven Igniter: A Complete Guide

Is your LG oven failing to heat up? Perhaps you notice a faint gas smell but no flame, or your food cooks unevenly. These are classic signs of a faulty oven igniter. When your LG oven igniter stops working correctly, your oven cannot light the gas to produce heat. Fixing this issue yourself can save you time and money.
