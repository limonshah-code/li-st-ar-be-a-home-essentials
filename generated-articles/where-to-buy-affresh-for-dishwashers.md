### Alternative Cleaning Solutions and Maintaining Your Dishwasher

While Affresh is an excellent choice, it is helpful to know about other options for dishwasher cleaning. Understanding maintenance routines also keeps your appliance running smoothly. Regular cleaning is crucial for optimal performance. You can combine different methods to achieve the best results.

Some people prefer natural alternatives for cleaning their dishwasher. White vinegar is a common household cleaner. You can place a cup of white vinegar on the top rack of an empty dishwasher. Run a hot cycle. The vinegar helps cut through grease and hard water deposits. Baking soda is another natural option. Sprinkle a cup of baking soda on the bottom of the empty dishwasher. Let it sit overnight, then run a short hot cycle. These methods are generally safe and effective for light cleaning.

However, natural solutions might not be as potent as specialized cleaners like Affresh for stubborn buildup. Affresh is specifically formulated to tackle the complex residues found in dishwashers. It also contains ingredients to neutralize odors more effectively. For a deeper, more thorough clean, a dedicated dishwasher cleaner is often superior. Beyond cleaning products, proper dishwasher maintenance extends its lifespan. Always scrape food off plates before loading them. This prevents large food particles from clogging filters. Regularly clean the filter at the bottom of your dishwasher. This collects food debris and can be easily removed and rinsed under water. Check your spray arms for blocked holes. Use a toothpick to clear any obstructions.

Running an empty hot water cycle once a month can also help. This flushes out minor residues before they build up. Using a rinse aid prevents water spots on dishes. It also aids in drying. Understanding what are good dishwashers and how to care for them ensures they last. Regular maintenance, combined with products like Affresh, ensures your dishwasher remains efficient and odor-free.
