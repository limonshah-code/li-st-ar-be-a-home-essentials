## Beyond Affresh: Comprehensive Whirlpool Dishwasher Maintenance

While Affresh is a fantastic product for deep cleaning, a comprehensive maintenance routine involves more than just tablets. To ensure your Whirlpool dishwasher performs at its peak, I suggest a few other simple steps. These actions complement the work of Affresh. They address areas that even a powerful cleaner might not fully reach. Regular attention to these details can prevent common issues and extend your appliance's life.

First, inspect and clean the dishwasher filter regularly. Many modern Whirlpool dishwashers have a removable filter, usually located at the bottom of the tub. Food particles, labels, and other debris can get trapped here. A clogged filter restricts water flow and can cause odors or leave grit on your dishes. Remove the filter, rinse it under running water, and use a soft brush to dislodge any stuck-on grime. This is a quick task that makes a big difference.

Next, pay attention to the spray arms. These rotating arms have small nozzles that spray water onto your dishes. If these nozzles get clogged with mineral deposits or food bits, the spray pattern becomes uneven. This leads to poorly cleaned dishes. Use a toothpick or a small brush to clear any blockages in the spray arm holes. Ensure the spray arms spin freely
