### The Importance of Correct Temperature Units for Air Frying Success

Using the correct temperature unit is critical for achieving perfect air frying results. I've learned that even a slight difference in temperature can significantly impact how food cooks. When your air fryer’s display doesn't match your recipe's unit, it can lead to undercooked or overcooked meals. This is why knowing if your Gourmia air fryer is in Celsius or Fahrenheit matters.

Imagine a recipe calls for 350°F, but your air fryer is set to 350°C. 350°C is extremely hot, equivalent to 662°F. Your food would burn almost instantly. Conversely, if a recipe needs 200°C (392°F) and your air fryer is on 200°F, your food will cook slowly and might not crisp up. It will likely remain undercooked. This highlights the importance of accurate unit interpretation.

If your Gourmia air fryer does not allow you to switch units, or if you prefer to follow recipes in a different unit, you will need to convert temperatures. There are simple formulas for this:

*   **Fahrenheit to Celsius:** C = (F - 32) * 5/9
*   **Celsius to Fahrenheit:** F = C * 9/5 + 32

For quick conversions, many online tools and smartphone apps are available. These can help you easily convert temperatures without doing the math yourself. Always double-check your conversions before setting your air fryer. Precision in temperature ensures delicious and safe food.

#### My Gourmia air fryer is stuck on one unit. What should I do?

If your Gourmia air fryer is stuck on one temperature unit, first re-read your user manual for the exact steps. Ensure you are pressing the correct button combination (often 'Temp' or 'Mode' button) for the specified duration. Try unplugging the air fryer for 5-10 minutes to reset it. If the issue persists, your model might not support unit changes, or it could have a technical fault.

#### How do I convert a recipe from Celsius to Fahrenheit (or vice versa)?

You can convert temperatures using simple formulas. To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply the Celsius temperature by 9/5 and then add 32 (F = C * 9/5 + 32). To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature and then multiply by 5/9 (C = (F - 32) * 5/9). Many online conversion tools and smartphone apps can also perform these calculations quickly.

#### Does changing the unit affect cooking time?

No, changing the temperature display unit from Celsius to Fahrenheit, or vice versa, does not affect the actual cooking time. The air fryer still operates at the same internal temperature. The unit change only alters how that temperature is shown on the screen. Cooking time depends on the food type, its size, and the recipe's recommended duration, not the display unit.

#### Why is my Gourmia air fryer temperature inaccurate?

Several factors can lead to perceived temperature inaccuracy. Ensure your Gourmia air fryer is properly preheated before adding food. Overcrowding the basket can also prevent even heat distribution. If you consistently experience significant temperature discrepancies, the appliance's sensor might be faulty. Consult your manual for troubleshooting or consider contacting Gourmia support.
