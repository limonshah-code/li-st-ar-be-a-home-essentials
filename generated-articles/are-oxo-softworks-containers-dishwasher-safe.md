## Why Top Rack Is Best: Preventing Damage and Warping

When cleaning OXO Softworks containers in the dishwasher, placement matters significantly. I always advise placing plastic items on the top rack. This practice is crucial for preventing damage and warping. The top rack of a dishwasher is farther away from the heating element. This element is usually at the bottom of the appliance. Temperatures on the lower rack can reach very high levels during the drying cycle. These intense temperatures can be too harsh for certain plastics.

Plastic materials, including those used in OXO containers, can soften when exposed to extreme heat. If a container is on the bottom rack, this heat can cause it to lose its shape. It may warp, twist, or shrink. A warped container will no longer seal properly. This defeats the purpose of airtight food storage. It also makes the container less effective for organization. You might find that lids no longer fit snugly. This problem reduces the lifespan of your OXO products. For general information on what items may not be suitable, refer to [what is not dishwasher safe](https://homeessentialsguide.com/what-is-not-dishwasher-safe).

The top rack provides a gentler washing and drying environment. Water sprays clean the items without exposing them to direct, prolonged heat. This preserves the integrity of the plastic. It helps your OXO containers maintain their original shape and functionality. Always ensure containers are spaced out. This allows water to circulate freely around each item. Proper spacing ensures a thorough clean without causing damage. Following this simple rule helps keep your OXO containers looking new and performing their best.

## Cleaning Stains and Odors from OXO Plastic

Even with careful use, plastic food containers can develop stains and odors. Tomato sauce, curries, and other vibrant foods often leave behind orange or yellow stains. Strong-smelling foods like garlic or onions can leave lingering odors. Fortunately, several effective methods exist for cleaning stains and odors from your OXO Softworks containers. I have found these techniques helpful in keeping my containers fresh. Addressing these issues promptly helps maintain the appearance and hygiene of your containers.

For stains, a common and effective remedy is a baking soda paste. Mix baking soda with a small amount of water to form a thick paste. Apply this paste directly to the stained area inside the container. Let it sit for several hours, or even overnight, for stubborn stains. The baking soda acts as a mild abrasive and a deodorizer. After the waiting period, scrub the area with a soft sponge or brush. Then, rinse the container thoroughly with warm water and soap. Repeat if necessary. This method often lifts even deep-set stains. You can explore more options for stain removal by reading about [how to clean stained plastic containers](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-stained-plastic-containers).

To combat odors, white vinegar is an excellent choice. Fill the container with a solution of equal parts white vinegar and water. Let it soak for at least 30 minutes, or longer for strong odors. The vinegar neutralizes smells. After soaking, empty the container and wash it with soap and water. Rinse thoroughly. Another trick for odor removal is to place crumpled newspaper inside the dry container. The newspaper absorbs odors. Sunlight can also help. Place the clean, empty container outside in direct sunlight for a few hours. The UV rays help to bleach stains and eliminate smells naturally.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid When Cleaning OXO Containers

Cleaning your OXO Softworks containers seems simple, but some common mistakes can reduce their lifespan or effectiveness. Avoiding these errors ensures your containers remain in top condition. I have made some of these mistakes myself in the past. Learning from them helps me take better care of my kitchen essentials. Understanding what not to do is just as important as knowing what to do. This prevents frustration and unnecessary replacement costs.

One major mistake is placing plastic OXO containers on the bottom rack of the dishwasher. The intense heat near the heating element can cause plastic to warp, melt, or become brittle. Always opt for the top rack, where temperatures are gentler. Another common error is not disassembling the lids. Many OXO lids have multiple parts, like gaskets or push-button mechanisms. Failing to take these apart means food particles and moisture can get trapped. This leads to unsanitary conditions, mold growth, and unpleasant odors. Always separate these components for a thorough clean.

Using abrasive scrubbers or harsh chemical cleaners is another mistake. Steel wool, rough sponges, or bleach-based cleaners can scratch the plastic surface. Scratches make the containers look cloudy and create areas where bacteria can hide. Stick to soft sponges or cloths and mild dish soap. Also, reassembling containers while they are still wet can cause problems. Trapped moisture leads to mildew and a stale smell. Ensure all parts are completely dry before putting them back together. By avoiding these common pitfalls, you will keep your OXO Softworks containers clean, clear, and durable.

## FAQ Section

### Are OXO POP container lids dishwasher safe?
Yes, OXO POP container lids are generally dishwasher safe. For best results, disassemble the lid first. Remove the push-button mechanism and the silicone gasket. Place all separated parts on the top rack of your dishwasher. This ensures thorough cleaning and prevents residue buildup.

### How do I clean the silicone gasket on OXO containers?
To clean the silicone gasket, gently pull it off the lid. Wash it by hand with warm, soapy water or place it on the top rack of your dishwasher. Ensure it is completely dry before reattaching to prevent mold or mildew growth. This keeps the seal effective.

### Can I put OXO glass containers in the dishwasher?
Yes, OXO glass containers are fully dishwasher safe. Glass is naturally more heat-resistant than plastic. You can typically place glass bases on either the top or bottom rack without concern for warping. Always clean the accompanying lids according to their specific material instructions.

### Why do my OXO plastic containers get cloudy?
Cloudiness often results from hard water deposits or etching from harsh detergents. To prevent this, consider using a rinse aid in your dishwasher. If handwashing, ensure thorough rinsing. For existing cloudiness, try soaking in white vinegar or scrubbing with a baking soda paste.

### Is the OXO salad spinner dishwasher safe?
Many OXO salad spinners are largely dishwasher safe. The clear bowl and spinner basket can usually go on the top rack. However, the lid, especially the pump mechanism, often requires handwashing. Check the specific product instructions for your model to confirm. You can find more details on [can you put OXO salad spinner in dishwasher](https://homeessentialsguide.com/can-you-put-oxo-salad-spinner-in-dishwasher).

### Can I use bleach to clean my OXO containers?
Avoid using bleach on plastic OXO containers. Bleach can degrade plastic over time, leading to discoloration, brittleness, and an unpleasant residue. For stubborn stains or odors, use natural alternatives like baking soda, vinegar, or lemon juice. These options are safer and just as effective.
