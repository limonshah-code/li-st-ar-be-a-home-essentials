### Curing Process

The curing process is the most important step for dishwasher safety. After applying your final coat, let the item air dry for 24 hours. This is the initial drying period. After 24 hours, the item will feel dry to the touch. However, it is not fully cured yet.

The true dishwasher-safe cure takes much longer. You must wait 28 days before washing the item in a dishwasher. I know this seems like a long time. But this extended period allows the Mod Podge to fully harden and bond. Washing it sooner can cause the finish to peel or bubble. Mark your calendar to avoid early washing. Hand wash items gently during this curing period if needed.
