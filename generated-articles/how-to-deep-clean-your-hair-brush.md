## Deep Clean Your Hair Brush for Healthier Hair

Have you ever looked closely at your hair brush? It gathers more than just loose strands. Your brush collects dirt, oil, product residue, dead skin cells, and bacteria. This hidden grime transfers back to your hair with every stroke. Regular cleaning is important, but a true deep clean for your hair brush goes further. It removes stubborn buildup and sanitizes the bristles and base. A clean brush means healthier, shinier hair and a cleaner scalp. It also makes your brush last longer.
