## Saving Your AirPods After a Washing Machine Accident

"Oh no, not the AirPods!" That sinking feeling hits hard when you pull your laundry from the washing machine, and there they are. It happens to many people, often due to a forgotten pocket check. Your beloved AirPods have just taken an unexpected, soapy swim. When AirPods go through the washing machine, swift action is absolutely important.

This guide will show you exactly what steps to take. We will cover immediate rescue, safe drying methods, and how to check for damage. We aim to help you bring your water-damaged AirPods back to life. Following these steps carefully increases their chances of survival. Let's work together to save your earbuds.

### Takeaway

*   **Act Immediately:** Retrieve AirPods from the washing machine and the case. Do not try to charge or power them on.
*   **Dry Thoroughly:** Gently wipe all surfaces with a lint-free cloth. Then, air dry or use silica gel for at least 72 hours.
*   **Avoid Heat:** Never use hair dryers, ovens, or microwaves. High heat causes more damage.
*   **Be Patient:** Wait several days before attempting to reconnect or charge them. This allows complete moisture evaporation.

### Clear, Concise Answer

If your AirPods go through the washing machine, immediately retrieve them and the case. Do not try to turn them on or charge them. Gently wipe them with a dry, lint-free cloth. Place them in a dry, open area for several days, possibly with a desiccant like silica gel, to allow all moisture to evaporate fully before testing.
