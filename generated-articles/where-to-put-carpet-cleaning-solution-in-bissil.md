### Where to Put Carpet Cleaning Solution in Bissell: The Direct Answer

You put carpet cleaning solution into the **clean water tank** of your Bissell machine. This tank is usually clear plastic and often has separate fill lines for water and concentrated cleaning solution. It is distinct from the dirty water collection tank.

### Understanding Your Bissell's Solution System

My Bissell carpet cleaner uses a clever two-tank system. This design helps keep things separate for effective cleaning. One tank holds the clean water and cleaning solution mixture. The other tank collects the dirty water vacuumed up from your carpet.

The clean water tank is crucial. This is where the magic happens for lifting dirt and stains. Without the right mixture in this tank, your carpets will not get a deep clean. Knowing how each tank works helps you use your machine correctly.

### Locating the Clean Water/Solution Tank on Your Bissell

Finding the correct tank on your Bissell is simple once you know what to look for. Most Bissell machines have a clear, removable tank. This tank sits either on top or at the front of the machine. It often has a handle for easy removal.

This tank is clearly different from the dirty water tank. The clean tank is where you mix water and solution. It will have clear labels or lines for filling. Always double-check your specific model's manual if you are unsure. This ensures you target the correct part.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Filling the Solution Tank

Filling your Bissell's clean water tank is a straightforward process. First, remove the clean water tank from your machine. You will usually find a latch or button to release it. Carry it to a sink for easy filling.

Next, you will see markings inside the tank. These markings guide you on how much water and solution to add. Pour hot tap water (not boiling) into the tank first, up to the designated water fill line. Then, add the concentrated Bissell cleaning solution. Pour the solution up to its specific fill line. Do not overfill either section. Once filled, securely replace the tank onto your Bissell machine.

### FAQ Section

#### 1. Can I use other brands of carpet cleaner in my Bissell?
I strongly recommend against using other brands of carpet cleaner in your Bissell. Bissell solutions are specifically formulated for their machines. Using non-Bissell formulas can lead to excessive suds, clogs, or damage to internal components. It may also void your machine's warranty. Always stick to Bissell-approved cleaning solutions for best results.

#### 2. Do I put solution in the same tank as water?
Yes, for most Bissell models, you put the cleaning solution directly into the clean water tank. This tank typically has separate fill lines. You fill hot tap water up to one line, then add the concentrated Bissell cleaning solution up to another specific line. The machine then mixes them as it cleans.

#### 3. What happens if I use too much solution?
Using too much cleaning solution can leave a sticky residue on your carpet fibers. This residue attracts dirt more quickly, making your carpets get dirty faster. It can also make your carpet feel stiff or hard after drying. Additionally, excessive suds can be difficult to rinse out, leading to a dull appearance.

#### 4. Can I just use water in my Bissell?
Yes, you can use just water in your Bissell machine. This is often recommended for a final rinse pass after a cleaning cycle with solution. Using plain water helps remove any leftover soap residue. For very light soil, plain water might offer some refresh, but it won't provide the deep cleaning power of a solution.

#### 5. How do I know which tank is for solution?
The tank for solution is typically the clean water tank. It is usually clear plastic and often located on the top or front of your Bissell machine. It will have clear fill lines or labels indicating where to add water and where to add concentrated cleaning solution. The dirty water tank is usually at the bottom or back and is solely for collecting extracted dirty water.

#### 6. Should I pre-mix the solution and water before putting it in the tank?
No, you should not pre-mix the solution and water outside the tank. For most Bissell machines, you fill the hot tap water directly into the clean water tank first, up to the marked line. Then, you add the concentrated Bissell cleaning solution to its own separate fill line within the same tank. The machine is designed to mix these components correctly as it operates.
