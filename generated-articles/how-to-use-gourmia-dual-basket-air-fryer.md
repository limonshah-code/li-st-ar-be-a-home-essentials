#### Tips for Optimal Basket Use

*   **Avoid Overcrowding:** Overfilling a basket reduces air circulation, leading to unevenly cooked and less crispy food. Always leave space for hot air to flow around the food. Cook in batches if necessary. This helps you achieve the best texture and even cooking.
*   **Shake or Flip:** For most foods, especially fries, wings, and vegetables, shaking or flipping them halfway through cooking is essential. This ensures all sides get exposed to the hot air. It leads to uniform browning and crispness. Some Gourmia models might even have a shake reminder function.
*   **Use Proper Liners (Optional):** While not always necessary, using parchment paper liners designed for air fryers can make cleanup easier. Ensure they are perforated to allow air circulation. Never use foil or parchment that covers the entire basket bottom, as this blocks airflow.

By following these strategies, your Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer becomes an incredibly powerful tool. It allows you to create diverse, perfectly cooked meals with maximum efficiency.

### Beyond Basics: Advanced Tips for Best Results

Once you are comfortable with the basic operation of your Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer, you can explore advanced techniques. These tips help you achieve even better results and expand your cooking repertoire. Air frying is more versatile than just heating frozen foods. You can truly elevate your meals.

#### Preheating for Perfection

Many people skip preheating, but it makes a big difference. Preheating your air fryer for 3-5 minutes at the desired cooking temperature helps ensure immediate crisping. It mimics the effect of a preheated oven. This leads to a better texture, especially for fried foods like chicken wings or breaded items. Food cooks more evenly and becomes crispier from the start.

Without preheating, food often starts cooking slowly. This can result in a softer texture instead of the desired crisp. Always remember to preheat for optimal results.

#### Achieving Maximum Crispness and Even Cooking

*   **Little to No Oil:** While air fryers use less oil than deep frying, a light spray of oil can enhance crispness and flavor. Use an oil mister or spray bottle for even coating. Avoid aerosol sprays with propellants that can damage the non-stick coating. Olive oil, avocado oil, or vegetable oil work well.
*   **Don't Overcrowd:** This is crucial. Air needs to circulate around each piece of food. If you pack the basket too full, the hot air cannot reach all surfaces. This results in steamed rather than fried food. Cook in smaller batches if necessary. This guarantees crispy, evenly cooked results every time.
*   **Shake or Flip Regularly:** For most foods, especially smaller items like fries or nuggets, shaking the basket halfway through cooking is essential. For larger items like chicken breasts, flipping them ensures even browning on both sides. This simple action prevents one side from becoming overly crisp while the other remains soft.
*   **Consider Food Placement:** For items like fish fillets or larger cuts, arrange them in a single layer. This maximizes exposure to hot air. If cooking multiple items, ensure there's space between them. Proper placement contributes to superior results.

By implementing these advanced tips, you will consistently achieve restaurant-quality results from your Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer. Your meals will be perfectly crispy and cooked through.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### How do I clean my Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer?

To clean your Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer, always unplug it and let it cool first. Remove the baskets and crisper trays. Wash them by hand with warm, soapy water or place them in the dishwasher if they are dishwasher-safe. Wipe the exterior with a damp cloth. Avoid submerging the main unit in water. This routine keeps your air fryer clean and functioning well.

#### Can I cook different foods at different temperatures in each basket?

Yes, absolutely! This is the primary benefit of a dual basket air fryer. You can set independent temperatures and cooking times for each basket. Use the "Sync Cook" function to ensure both dishes finish cooking at the same moment. This allows you to prepare a complete meal, like chicken in one basket and vegetables in the other, ready together.

#### Do I need to preheat my Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer?

Preheating your Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer is highly recommended for best results. Preheating for 3-5 minutes at your desired cooking temperature helps the food start cooking immediately upon entry. This leads to a crispier exterior and more even cooking. It mimics a preheated oven, giving your food a better texture.

#### How do I get food crispy in my air fryer?

To get food crispy, avoid overcrowding the baskets. Ensure there is enough space for hot air to circulate around each piece. Lightly spray food with oil, and remember to shake the baskets or flip the food halfway through cooking. Preheating also helps achieve maximum crispness. These steps ensure golden, crunchy results.

#### What does the Sync Cook function do?

The Sync Cook function on your Gourmia Dual Basket Air Fryer allows you to cook two different foods that require different times and temperatures. You set each basket's parameters, and the air fryer automatically staggers their start times. This means both dishes will finish cooking simultaneously. It is ideal for complete meals where sides and main courses need to be ready together.

#### Can I put water in my Gourmia air fryer for cleaning?

No, you should not put water directly into the main unit of your Gourmia air fryer. The electrical components are not designed for direct water contact. You can use water to clean the removable baskets and trays, as they are often dishwasher-safe or designed for hand washing. Always keep the main unit dry and wipe it clean with a damp cloth.
