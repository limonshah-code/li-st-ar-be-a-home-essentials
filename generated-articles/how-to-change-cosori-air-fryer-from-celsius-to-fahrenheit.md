### Step-by-Step Guide: Changing Celsius to Fahrenheit on Cosori Display

Changing the temperature unit on your Cosori air fryer is simple. The exact steps vary slightly by model. Here, I will provide a general guide that covers most Cosori air fryers. Follow these steps carefully for a smooth transition.

#### For Models with Physical Buttons:

1.  **Locate the Temperature Button:** Look for a button on your control panel labeled "TEMP," "°C/°F," or sometimes just "M" (Mode). This button controls temperature adjustments.
2.  **Power On (If Needed):** Ensure your Cosori air fryer is plugged in and powered on. The display should be active. You do not need to start a cooking cycle.
3.  **Press and Hold:** Press and hold the identified "TEMP" or "°C/°F" button for about 3 to 5 seconds.
    *   You might hear a beep.
    *   The temperature unit on the display should change from "°C" to "°F."
4.  **Confirm Change:** Release the button. The new unit, Fahrenheit, should now be visible. The air fryer saves this setting.

#### For Models with Touchscreen Displays (e.g., Cosori Smart WiFi Air Fryer):

1.  **Activate Display:** Ensure the air fryer is plugged in and the display is active. You may need to press the power button once.
2.  **Look for the Unit Icon:** On a touchscreen, look for a small icon or text indicating the temperature unit (e.g., "°C" or "°F"). This is often next to the temperature setting itself.
3.  **Tap or Press and Hold:** Tap the temperature unit icon. On some models, a quick tap is enough. For others, you might need to press and hold for a few seconds.
4.  **Verify:** The display will switch to the other unit. You will see "°F" if it was "°C." The change is immediate.

If these steps do not work, your specific model might have a unique method. Some older or less common models might require different button combinations. Always check your user manual for the most accurate information. You can often find digital manuals on the Cosori website by searching for your model number.

Sometimes, changing the temperature unit on your Cosori air fryer does not go as planned. Do not worry. Many common issues have simple solutions. I have encountered a few myself, and they are usually easy to fix.

First, **check the power supply**. Make sure your air fryer is fully plugged in and powered on. A display that is off or flickering might prevent any setting changes. Try unplugging the air fryer for a minute, then plugging it back in. This often resets the system.

Second, **ensure you are pressing the correct button for long enough**. A common mistake is a quick press instead of a sustained hold. Most Cosori models require you to hold the temperature unit button for 3 to 5 seconds. Releasing too soon will not register the change. Experiment with the duration of your press.

Third, **confirm your specific Cosori model's instructions**. Not all Cosori models are identical. While most follow similar patterns, some might have unique methods. For instance, some older models might have a different button combination. Your user manual is the best resource. If you lost it, search the Cosori website with your model number. Many online forums or user communities also offer specific model advice.

Fourth, **look for a factory reset option**. Some advanced Cosori models might have a way to reset all settings to default. If you have tried everything else, a factory reset might fix a software glitch. Be aware this will clear any custom presets you have saved. Check your manual before attempting a reset.

Finally, if none of these steps work, **contact Cosori customer support**. They can provide specific troubleshooting steps for your exact model. They might also be aware of any known issues. Describe your problem clearly. Having your model number ready will help them assist you faster.
