## Hand Washing Your Our Place Pan: The Recommended Method

Hand washing is the gold standard for cleaning your Our Place Pan. This method protects the non-stick ceramic coating. It ensures the pan stays in excellent condition for many years. It is a simple process that requires minimal effort. I find it very quick and easy to do right after cooking.

First, let the pan cool down completely after use. Do not put a hot pan under cold water. This temperature shock can warp the pan or damage its coating. Once cool, use warm water and a small amount of mild dish soap. I always use a soft sponge or a non-abrasive dishcloth. Avoid steel wool or scrubbing pads. These can scratch the delicate surface.

Gently wipe down the interior and exterior of the pan. Most food slides right off the non-stick surface with ease. If you have stubborn bits, let the pan soak for a few minutes with warm, soapy water. Then, gently scrub again. Rinse thoroughly with warm water. Finally, dry the pan immediately with a soft towel. Do not let it air dry, as water spots can form. This simple routine keeps your Our Place Pan ready for your next culinary adventure. This gentle approach is key to longevity.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Hand Washing:

1.  **Cool Down:** Always let your Our Place Pan cool completely before washing.
2.  **Add Water and Soap:** Fill the pan with warm water and add a few drops of mild dish soap.
3.  **Soak (If Needed):** For stubborn food residue, let the pan soak for 5-10 minutes.
4.  **Gentle Scrub:** Use a soft sponge or cloth to wipe away food and grease. Do not use abrasive scrubbers.
5.  **Rinse:** Rinse the pan thoroughly under warm running water.
6.  **Dry Immediately:** Dry the pan completely with a soft, clean towel. Do not air dry. This prevents water marks and potential damage.

## Our Place Pan Maintenance Tips

Maintaining your Our Place Pan well involves simple habits that make a big difference. These practices ensure the pan delivers consistent performance. I always make sure to follow these guidelines. It helps keep my pan looking new and cooking like a dream.

*   **Seasoning (Optional but helpful):** While not strictly required like cast iron, some users report occasional light seasoning helps. Rub a tiny amount of cooking oil (like avocado oil) on the clean, dry non-stick surface with a paper towel. Heat on low for 30 seconds, then wipe excess. This can help maintain slipperiness.
*   **Avoid Aerosol Sprays:** Cooking sprays can build up residue that is hard to remove. This residue can become sticky and interfere with the non-stick surface. Use a small amount of liquid oil or butter instead.
*   **Clean Immediately After Cooling:** Do not leave food sitting in the pan for long periods. Acidic foods can be particularly harsh on coatings. Clean the pan as soon as it cools down to prevent staining and sticking.
*   **Store Properly:** Place pan protectors or soft cloths between stacked pans. This prevents scratches on the non-stick surface. Many people hang their pans. This is an excellent storage option.
*   **Check for Wear:** Regularly inspect your pan for signs of wear. Look for scratches, chipping, or areas where food begins to stick more often. Address these issues quickly if possible, or consider retiring the pan if performance significantly declines.

## FAQs About Our Place Pans and Dishwashers

### Can the Our Place Always Pan go in the dishwasher?
No, the Our Place Always Pan is generally not recommended for dishwasher use. Its ceramic non-stick coating can degrade from high heat, harsh detergents, and abrasive cycles. Our Place advises hand washing to preserve the pan's non-stick properties and extend its lifespan.

### What happens if I put my Our Place Pan in the dishwasher?
Putting your Our Place Pan in the dishwasher can damage its non-stick ceramic coating. This may lead to food sticking more easily over time. The pan's exterior might also discolor, and the non-stick properties can weaken, making the pan less effective and enjoyable to use.

### How do I properly clean my Our Place Pan by hand?
To hand wash your Our Place Pan, let it cool completely. Use warm water, mild dish soap, and a soft sponge or cloth. Gently wipe the pan's interior and exterior. For stubborn food, soak the pan for a few minutes. Rinse thoroughly and dry immediately with a soft towel.

### Are any Our Place products dishwasher safe?
Some Our Place accessories, like specific steamer baskets or tools, might be dishwasher safe. Always check the specific product's care instructions on the Our Place website or packaging. Their main pans, such as the Always Pan and Perfect Pot, usually require hand washing.

### How can I make my Our Place Pan last longer?
To extend your Our Place Pan's life, hand wash it gently after each use. Use non-metal utensils like silicone or wood. Avoid high heat and aerosol cooking sprays. Store the pan with protectors or cloths between stacked items to prevent scratches.

### Why is my Our Place Pan losing its non-stick?
Your Our Place Pan might be losing its non-stick properties due to improper care. This includes using metal utensils, high heat cooking, using abrasive cleaners, or frequent dishwasher use. Residue buildup from cooking sprays can also diminish its non-stick performance.
