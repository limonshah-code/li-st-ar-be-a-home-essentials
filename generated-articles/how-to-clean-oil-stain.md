## Mastering Oil Stain Removal: Your Comprehensive Guide

Oil stains are a common household challenge. They can appear on clothes, kitchen counters, or garage floors. Dealing with these greasy marks can feel daunting. But do not worry; cleaning oil stains is possible with the right approach. This guide shares effective strategies for removing oil stains from various surfaces. We will cover immediate actions, specific methods for different materials, and even tips to prevent future spills.

### Takeaway:
*   Act fast: Treat oil stains immediately for best results.
*   Blot, don't rub: Absorb excess oil before cleaning.
