### Takeaway

For pristine dishes and a long-lasting Bosch dishwasher, remember these key points:

*   **Prioritize Quality:** Always opt for high-quality, enzyme-based detergent tablets or pods.
*   **Rinse Aid is Essential:** Use a good rinse aid to prevent spots and aid drying.
*   **Know Your Water:** Adjust detergent use based on your water hardness.
*   **Avoid Liquids:** Limit liquid or gel detergents; they are less effective.
*   **Load Properly:** Correct loading ensures water and detergent reach all items.

### Bosch Dishwasher Detergent: The Direct Answer

Bosch strongly recommends using high-quality, enzyme-based detergent tablets or pods for their dishwashers. These formulations offer superior cleaning power and protect the appliance. They advise combining these detergents with a good rinse aid for spotless drying results. This approach ensures your dishes are clean and your machine functions well over time.
