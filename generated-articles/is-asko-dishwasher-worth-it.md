### Common Asko Dishwasher Issues and Maintenance: What to Expect

Even premium appliances can have issues. Understanding common problems helps manage expectations. Asko dishwashers are reliable, but some things can still happen. Knowing about them helps you keep your appliance running well.

One common concern for any dishwasher is drainage. If water remains in the tub, it indicates a problem. This might be a clogged filter or a blocked drain hose. Regular cleaning of the filter helps prevent this. It is easy to do.

Sometimes, dishes might not dry completely. This could be due to improper loading. It might also be related to the drying cycle selected. Asko's Turbo Drying system is very efficient. However, plastics sometimes need extra air dry time. If [your Asko dishwasher is not drying](https://homeessentialsguide.com/why-is-my-asko-dishwasher-not-drying) well, check the loading first.

Noise levels are usually low. If your Asko becomes noisy, it could signal a problem. This might be due to a foreign object in the pump. It could also be a worn part. Always consult the manual for troubleshooting.

Odors can develop in any dishwasher. This usually happens if food particles build up. Regular cleaning cycles with a dishwasher cleaner help. Also, ensure you regularly clean the filter. If [your Asko dishwasher smells](https://homeessentialsguide.com/why-does-my-asko-dishwasher-smell), a deep clean is often the answer.

Maintenance for an Asko dishwasher is straightforward. The most important step is cleaning the filter. Asko filters are easy to remove and wash. Do this regularly, perhaps once a month. This prevents clogs and maintains cleaning performance.

Wipe down the interior and exterior periodically. Check the spray arms for blockages. Make sure they spin freely. Using the correct type and amount of detergent is also important. These simple steps ensure your Asko dishwasher provides many years of trouble-free operation.

If serious issues arise, professional service is recommended. Asko has a network of authorized technicians. They can diagnose and fix complex problems. The robust build makes repairs worthwhile. The parts are designed to be replaced, not the whole machine.
