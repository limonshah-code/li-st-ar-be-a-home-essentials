## How To Wash Canvas Sperrys in Your Washing Machine

Do your favorite canvas Sperrys look less than their best? Years of wear can leave them looking dull or dirty. Many people wonder if they can simply toss their canvas shoes into the washing machine. I understand that concern. Good news: you can wash canvas Sperrys in a washing machine. This method is effective for refreshing your footwear. This guide will walk you through the process step-by-step. You will learn how to safely and effectively clean canvas Sperrys. We cover everything from preparing your shoes to drying them properly. By the end, your Sperrys will look fresh and ready for new adventures. This detailed article gives you all the information you need. You will feel confident caring for your favorite canvas boat shoes.

### Takeaway:

*   Always remove laces and insoles before washing.
*   Pre-treat stains for best results.
*   Use a mesh laundry bag to protect shoes.
*   Wash on a cold, gentle cycle with mild detergent.
*   Air dry your canvas Sperrys away from direct heat.

### Concise Answer:

You can wash canvas Sperrys in a washing machine using a gentle cycle with cold water and mild detergent. Pre-treat stains, remove laces and insoles, and place shoes in a mesh bag for protection
