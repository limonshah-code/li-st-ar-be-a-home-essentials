### The Role of the Flow-Through Heater

Bosch dishwashers use a special type of heater. It is called a flow-through heater. This heater is compact and efficient. Water flows directly through it as it heats up. This design is different from submerged heating coils. Submerged coils sit at the bottom of the tub. They are visible.

A flow-through heater is hidden. It also makes less noise. This heater warms the water as it passes through. This ensures water reaches the spray arms at the right temperature. This design prevents food particles from baking onto the element. This helps keep the dishwasher clean and free from odors. It also means no plastic items will melt on a hot coil.
