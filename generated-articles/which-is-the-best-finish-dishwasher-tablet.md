### Maximizing Performance of Your Finish Dishwasher Tablets

#### Proper Loading Techniques

Loading your dishwasher correctly is crucial. Do not overcrowd the racks. Overcrowding prevents water and detergent from reaching all surfaces. This leads to dirty spots on dishes. Place larger items in the back or sides. Ensure they do not block spray arms.

Angle plates to allow water flow. Cups and bowls should face downwards. Separate silverware to prevent nesting. Proper loading lets the Finish tablet work efficiently. It ensures every item gets thoroughly cleaned. This simple step improves results greatly.

#### Regular Dishwasher Maintenance

A clean dishwasher cleans dishes better. Food particles and grease can build up inside. This residue affects performance and can cause odors. Regularly clean your dishwasher's filter. This prevents clogs and improves water circulation.

Run an empty cycle with a dishwasher cleaner once a month. Finish makes specific dishwasher cleaners for this purpose. This removes grease and limescale buildup. A clean machine helps your Finish tablet dissolve and clean effectively. It ensures your dishes come out spotless.
