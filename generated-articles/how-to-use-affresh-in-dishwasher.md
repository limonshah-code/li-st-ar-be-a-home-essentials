## Step-by-Step Guide: How To Use Affresh In Dishwasher (Empty Load Method)

Using Affresh to clean your dishwasher is a simple process. Follow these steps for the best results. This method cleans your appliance thoroughly.

### Step 1: Prepare Your Dishwasher

First, make sure your dishwasher is empty. Remove all dishes, silverware, and any large food scraps. You do not want any items inside during the cleaning cycle. This ensures the Affresh tablet can work on the machine's interior only. It also prevents any cleaning agent from touching your dishes.

### Step 2: Place the Affresh Tablet

You have two main options for placing the tablet.
*   **Option A (Detergent Dispenser):** Open your dishwasher's main detergent dispenser. Place one Affresh tablet inside. Close the dispenser door. This is the most common method.
*   **Option B (Bottom Rack):** If your dispenser door does not close, you can place the tablet directly on the bottom rack. Put it in a secure spot, away from the drain. This ensures it dissolves properly during the cycle.

Avoid unwrapping the tablet. It is designed to dissolve as is. Do not use regular dish detergent during this process. The Affresh tablet is all you need.

### Step 3: Select the Right Cycle

Close your dishwasher door. Select the hottest available wash cycle. Many dishwashers have a "heavy wash," "pots and pans," or "sanitize" cycle. A hot water cycle helps activate the cleaning agents in the Affresh tablet. This provides a deep clean. Do not use a rinse-only or quick wash cycle. These cycles may not be long enough or hot enough.

### Step 4: Start the Dishwasher

Start your dishwasher. Let the full cycle complete. The Affresh tablet will dissolve. It will work to break down mineral buildup and grime. You will notice a fresh, clean smell once the cycle finishes. Running a full cycle is important. It ensures all components get proper cleaning.

Once the cycle is done, your dishwasher is ready for use. It should look and smell much cleaner. This simple routine keeps your machine in top condition. Affresh tablets contain a powerful, concentrated cleaning formula. This formula is designed to scrub away tough grime. It tackles mineral buildup and food residues that accumulate over time. ## Optimal Frequency and Additional Maintenance Tips

Regular cleaning with Affresh keeps your dishwasher performing its best. I recommend using one Affresh tablet once a month. This schedule helps prevent significant buildup. It also keeps odors at bay. In homes with hard water, you might consider using it every three weeks. Hard water areas tend to accumulate mineral deposits faster.

Between Affresh treatments, other simple maintenance tasks are important. These tasks prolong the life of your dishwasher. They also improve its daily cleaning power.

### Checking and Cleaning the Filter

Many modern dishwashers have a removable filter. This filter traps food particles. If it gets clogged, your dishes will not get clean. Bad odors can also come from a dirty filter.
*   **Locate the filter:** It is usually at the bottom of the dishwasher, under the spray arm.
*   **Remove it:** Twist or pull to detach the filter, following your manual's instructions.
*   **Rinse it:** Hold the filter under warm running water. Use a soft brush to scrub away any trapped food or grease.
*   **Reinstall it:** Put the clean filter back in its place securely. I try to do this weekly.

### Inspecting Spray Arms

The spray arms have small holes that jet water onto your dishes. These holes can get clogged with food bits or mineral deposits.
*   **Check for blockages:** Look at the small holes on the spray arms. Use a toothpick or thin wire to clear any obstructions.
*   **Ensure free movement:** Spin the spray arms with your hand. They should rotate freely without hitting anything.

### Wiping Down the Door Seal

The rubber gasket around the dishwasher door can collect grime. This area can harbor mold and mildew.
*   **Clean the seal:** Use a damp cloth with a mild cleaner or vinegar. Wipe down the entire door seal.
*   **Dry it:** Ensure the seal is dry to prevent mildew growth.

## FAQs About Using Affresh in Dishwasher

### Can I use Affresh washing machine cleaner in my dishwasher?
No, you should not use Affresh washing machine cleaner in your dishwasher. These products have different formulations. Each is designed for the specific environment and type of grime found in each appliance. Using the wrong cleaner can be ineffective or even cause damage. Always use the Affresh product specifically for dishwashers.

### How often should I use Affresh in my dishwasher?
For optimal performance and freshness, you should use Affresh dishwasher cleaner once a month. Regular monthly cleaning prevents significant buildup of food particles, grease, and hard water minerals. If you have very hard water, you might consider using it every three weeks. This helps maintain a consistently clean machine.

### Where exactly do I put the Affresh tablet in the dishwasher?
You have two primary options for placing the Affresh tablet. You can place one tablet in the main detergent dispenser. Then, close the dispenser door. Alternatively, you can place the tablet directly on the bottom rack of the empty dishwasher. Ensure it is in a spot where it will not fall through the rack.

### What if my dishwasher still smells bad after using Affresh?
If odors persist after an Affresh cycle, check the dishwasher's filter and drain area. Remove and manually clean the filter thoroughly. Inspect the drain for any large food particles or obstructions. You might need to repeat the Affresh cycle. Sometimes, a very dirty machine requires more than one treatment to fully remove stubborn odors.

### Does Affresh clean the dishwasher filter?
Affresh helps to clean the dishwasher filter by dissolving grime and buildup. However, for best results, manually remove and clean your dishwasher filter regularly. While Affresh assists, physical scrubbing ensures all trapped food particles are removed. This ensures proper drainage and prevents odors.

### Is Affresh safe for all dishwasher brands?
Yes, Affresh dishwasher cleaner is generally safe for all major dishwasher brands. It is designed to be universally compatible with stainless steel, plastic, and other common dishwasher materials. Whether you have a Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, GE, or another brand, you can use Affresh with confidence. Always follow the product's instructions.
