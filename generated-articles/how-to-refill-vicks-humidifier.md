## How to Safely Refill Your Vicks Humidifier

**Introduction:**
Do you find yourself wondering how to correctly refill your Vicks humidifier? Many people ask this question. A well-maintained humidifier provides moisture to your air. It helps ease coughs and congestion. Proper refilling is a key step in humidifier care. This article provides clear instructions. I will share tips for keeping your humidifier working well. You will learn about water types, best practices, and simple troubleshooting.

**Takeaway:**
*   Always unplug your Vicks humidifier before refilling the water tank.
*   Remove the water tank, turn it upside down, and unscrew the cap.
*   Fill the tank with cool, fresh water, then securely replace the cap.
*   Place the filled tank back on the base and plug in the unit to resume operation.

**Concise Answer:**
To refill your Vicks humidifier, first unplug it for safety. Remove the water tank from the base and unscrew the cap. Fill the tank with cool, fresh water. Make sure to choose distilled or demineralized water if possible. Securely replace the cap and place the tank back on the base. Finally, plug in the humidifier and turn it on.
