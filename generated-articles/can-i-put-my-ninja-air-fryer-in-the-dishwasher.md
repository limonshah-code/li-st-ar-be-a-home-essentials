## Understanding Your Ninja Air Fryer Components

To answer "Can I put my Ninja Air Fryer in the dishwasher?" we must first understand its parts. Your air fryer is more than just a single unit. It has several distinct components, each with different cleaning needs. Knowing what each part is made of helps you clean it safely. Improper cleaning can damage your appliance. It can also create safety risks. I want to help you keep your kitchen safe and your appliances working well.

### The Main Unit and Heating Element

The main body of your Ninja Air Fryer holds the electrical components. This includes the fan and the heating element. This part should never be submerged in water. It must never go into a dishwasher. Water can cause electrical damage. It creates a serious shock hazard. Always keep this part dry when cleaning. Use only a damp cloth for its exterior.

### The Basket and Crisper Plate

These are the parts where your food cooks. The basket holds the food, and the crisper plate sits inside the basket. Both are typically made from metal with a non-stick coating. Ninja designs many of these parts to be dishwasher safe. Their non-stick surface helps food release easily. It also makes cleaning easier. Always verify this information in your manual.

### Other Accessories and Racks

Some Ninja Air Fryer models come with extra accessories. These might include multi-layer racks or skewers. These items also often feature non-stick coatings or durable metal construction. Many of these smaller accessories are also dishwasher safe. They usually fit well on the top rack of your dishwasher. Always check for specific care instructions for each accessory. This prevents accidental damage.
