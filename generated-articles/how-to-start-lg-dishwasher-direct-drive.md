#### The Standard Start Procedure

### Troubleshooting: When Your LG Dishwasher Won't Start

Sometimes, your LG Direct Drive dishwasher may not start. This can be frustrating. Many starting problems have simple solutions. Do not panic if your machine does not respond. A few checks can often fix the issue quickly.

Common issues include power problems, door latch issues, or activated locks. We will cover these common reasons. You can get your dishwasher running again. This helps you avoid calling for service.

#### Power Supply and Breaker Checks

The most common reason a dishwasher will not start is no power. First, check if the dishwasher is plugged in correctly. Make sure the outlet works. Then, check your home's circuit breaker box. Find the breaker labeled for your kitchen or dishwasher. It might have tripped.

If the breaker is tripped, flip it off, then on again. This restores power. If the breaker trips often, there might be a wiring problem. In that case, contact an electrician. Always ensure power before checking other things.

#### Door Latch and Child Lock Issues

A common reason for no start is an unlatched door. The door must be completely closed and latched. Press firmly on the door until you hear a click. If the latch is broken, the dishwasher will not start. Inspect the latch for damage.

Many LG dishwashers have a "Child Lock" feature. This prevents accidental starts. If activated, the control panel may show a lock icon. To disable it, press and hold the "Child Lock" button for a few seconds. Your manual will confirm the specific button. #### Deciphering Error Codes

LG dishwashers display error codes when problems occur. These codes appear on the digital display. Common codes include "LE" (motor error), "OE" (drain error), or "IE" (water inlet error). Look up the specific code in your dishwasher's manual. The manual explains what each code means. It also provides steps to fix the problem.

For example, an "IE" error means no water is entering. You might need to check the water supply line. Some errors require a reset. Turn off the power, wait a few minutes, then turn it back on. ### Maintaining Your LG Direct Drive Dishwasher for Optimal Performance

Regular maintenance keeps your LG Direct Drive dishwasher working well. A well-maintained machine cleans dishes better. It also lasts longer. Simple cleaning steps can prevent many future problems. Make these tasks part of your routine.

#### Regular Filter Cleaning

Your LG dishwasher has a filter at the bottom. This filter traps food particles. It prevents them from recirculating onto your dishes. Clean this filter regularly, ideally once a week or every few washes. Remove the bottom rack. Twist or pull out the filter assembly.

Rinse the filter under running water. Use a soft brush to remove stubborn debris. Make sure no food bits remain. Reinstall the filter securely. A dirty filter leads to poor cleaning and odors. Clean filters mean sparkling dishes.

#### Deep Cleaning the Interior

Periodically, deep clean your dishwasher's interior. Food particles and detergent residue can build up. This causes odors and reduces efficiency. Use a dishwasher cleaner or a simple mixture of vinegar and baking soda. Place a cup of white vinegar in an empty dishwasher. Run a hot water cycle.

After the vinegar cycle, sprinkle baking soda on the bottom. Run a short, hot cycle again. This helps remove odors and stains. Wipe down the door gasket and edges with a damp cloth. Clean these areas to prevent mold. A clean interior gives you clean dishes.

### FAQ Section

#### What does "Direct Drive" mean for an LG dishwasher?
"Direct Drive" means the motor connects directly to the pump. There are no belts. This design makes the dishwasher very quiet during operation. It also reduces wear on parts. This often leads to a longer appliance life. Direct Drive motors are known for efficiency.

#### Why won't my LG dishwasher start when I press the button?
Several reasons can cause this. Check if the dishwasher has power. Ensure the door is fully closed and latched. Look for a child lock feature activated on the control panel. Also, check for any error codes displayed. These common issues stop a cycle from starting.

#### How do I select the best cycle for my dishes?
Choose a cycle based on how dirty your dishes are. "Normal" works for everyday loads. "Heavy" is for baked-on food or pots. "Auto" or "Sensor" cycles adjust based on soil level. Use "Delicate" for fragile items. Selecting the right cycle saves water and energy.

#### Can I use any detergent in my LG Direct Drive dishwasher?
No, you must use automatic dishwasher detergent. Do not use liquid dish soap meant for handwashing. Hand soap produces too many suds, which can damage the dishwasher. Always check the detergent dispenser for proper filling. Use rinse aid for spot-free drying.

#### How do I know if the child lock is on?
If the child lock is on, you will usually see a lock icon or a specific light on the control panel. The buttons will not respond when you press them. To turn it off, press and hold the "Child Lock" button for a few seconds. The exact method is in your dishwasher's manual.
