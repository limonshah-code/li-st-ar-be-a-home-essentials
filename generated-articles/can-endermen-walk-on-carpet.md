**Q1: Can Endermen pick up carpet blocks?**

Yes, Endermen can pathfind over carpet. Their AI treats carpet like any other solid block when determining walkable paths. Carpet does not create an obstacle or a gap in their pathfinding. They will walk across it as if it were a normal floor block.
