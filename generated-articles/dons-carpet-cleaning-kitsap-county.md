### Step 1: Pre-Inspection

Before any cleaning begins, a technician performs a pre-inspection. This is a very important first step. They walk through the areas you want cleaned. ### Step 2: Pre-Treatment

After the inspection, the technician applies a pre-treatment solution. This solution helps loosen dirt and break down tough stains. It sits on the carpet for a few minutes. This allows it to work its magic before the main cleaning begins. This step is crucial for achieving a deep clean, especially in high-traffic areas.

Different stains might need different pre-treatment products. For example, a pet stain needs a different solution than a grease stain. The technicians know which products to use for the best results. This targeted approach helps lift even stubborn marks effectively.

### Step 3: Hot Water Extraction (Steam Cleaning)

The main cleaning method used by Dons is hot water extraction, often called steam cleaning. This is a highly effective way to deep clean carpets. They use powerful equipment that injects hot water and a cleaning solution deep into the carpet fibers. Almost immediately, the machine extracts the water, along with the dirt and grime.

This method does not actually use steam. It uses hot water under high pressure. This helps to sanitize the carpet and remove allergens, bacteria, and dust mites. The powerful vacuum then pulls most of the moisture out. This leaves your carpets clean and only slightly damp. It dries relatively quickly.

### Step 4: Post-Treatment and Grooming

After the main cleaning, the technician may apply a post-treatment if needed. This could be a stain protector or an odor neutralizer. They then groom the carpet. This involves raking the carpet fibers. Grooming helps the carpet dry evenly and can restore its natural pile. It also gives the carpet a uniform, freshly cleaned appearance.

This step ensures your carpet looks its best once it dries. It also helps prevent matting as the carpet dries.

### Step 5: Final Inspection

The final step is a post-cleaning inspection with you. The technician walks through the cleaned areas again. They want to make sure you are happy with the results.
