## How To Clean FCW Sensor for Optimal Vehicle Safety

However, dirt, grime, and debris can compromise its performance. Keeping your FCW sensor clean is vital for its proper function. A dirty sensor can lead to false alarms or, worse, failure to detect real hazards. This article will guide you on how to clean an FCW sensor effectively and safely.
