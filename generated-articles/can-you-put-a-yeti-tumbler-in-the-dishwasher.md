### FAQ Section

**Can all Yeti products go in the dishwasher?**
Most current Yeti Rambler drinkware products are dishwasher safe. This includes tumblers, bottles, mugs, and jugs. However, always double-check the specific care instructions for your product. Older models or certain specialty items might require hand washing. I always recommend checking the product tag or Yeti's website.

**Does dishwashing damage the Yeti finish?**
No, modern Yeti products feature a durable Duracoat™ finish. This finish resists fading, peeling, and cracking. Dishwashing on the top rack with standard detergent is safe for the finish. Avoid abrasive cleaners or extremely hot cycles on the bottom rack. These could potentially cause premature wear over a long period.

**Is it okay to put Yeti lids on the bottom rack?**
It is best to place all Yeti lid components on the top rack of the dishwasher. The bottom rack can have higher heat and more powerful water jets. These conditions might potentially warp plastic parts or damage rubber gaskets over time. The top rack provides a gentler clean, protecting the integrity of your lid.

**How often should I clean my Yeti tumbler?**
You should clean your Yeti tumbler after every use. This prevents bacterial growth and odor buildup. If you use it daily, wash it daily. This ensures proper hygiene and maintains the taste of your beverages. Both hand washing and dishwashing are suitable for regular cleaning.

**What if my Yeti tumbler gets a strange smell after washing?**
A strange smell usually indicates trapped moisture or residue. Make sure to fully disassemble the lid, including removing gaskets and the MagSlider, before washing. Allow all parts to air dry completely before reassembling. For stubborn odors, a mixture of baking soda and vinegar can help neutralize them.
