### Routine Cleaning and Care

Even the best dishwasher needs cleaning. Miele recommends regular maintenance. This prevents build-up and ensures optimal performance.

*   **Clean the Filter:** Locate the filter at the bottom of the dishwasher. Remove it and rinse it under running water. This removes food particles. Do this once a month or as needed.
*   **Wipe the Door Seal:** Use a damp cloth to clean the rubber seal around the door. Food debris can get stuck here. This prevents odors and ensures a tight seal.
*   **Run a Maintenance Cycle:** Miele often has a specific cleaning program. Use a dishwasher cleaner. Run this cycle every few months. This cleans the internal components. It helps prevent mineral deposits. For more details, see [How To Clean Miele Dishwasher](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-miele-dishwasher).
*   **Check Spray Arms:** Ensure the spray arm nozzles are clear. Sometimes food bits can block them. Use a toothpick to clear any blockages.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are Miele dishwashers worth the price?
Miele dishwashers are a significant investment. They are known for their exceptional durability and long lifespan, often lasting 20 years. Their superior cleaning performance, quiet operation, and innovative features like AutoDos justify the cost for many users. The value comes from reliable performance and longevity.

### How long do Miele dishwashers last?
Miele designs its appliances to last a long time. They test their dishwashers for 10,000 operating hours. This equates to about 20 years of typical use. This commitment to durability is a major selling point. It means fewer replacements and long-term peace of mind.

### Do Miele dishwashers really dry dishes completely?
Yes, Miele dishwashers offer excellent drying performance. Many models feature SensorDry and AutoOpen drying technology. SensorDry detects moisture levels and adjusts drying time. AutoOpen drying automatically opens the door after the cycle. This releases steam, ensuring dishes are thoroughly dry.

### What is AutoDos and how does it work?
AutoDos is a Miele exclusive feature. It automatically dispenses the correct amount of detergent. It uses a PowerDisk, a compact disk filled with granular detergent. This disk lasts about 20 cycles. It ensures optimal cleaning for every load without you measuring detergent.

### How quiet are Miele dishwashers?
Miele dishwashers are among the quietest on the market. Many models operate at noise levels as low as 40 to 45 decibels (dB). This is quieter than a normal conversation. This makes them ideal for open-concept living spaces or for running overnight.

### How often should I clean my Miele dishwasher?
You should clean your Miele dishwasher regularly. Clean the filter at the bottom of the tub once a month. Wipe the door seal weekly to prevent grime build-up. Run a dedicated machine cleaning cycle with a Miele cleaner every few months. This maintains performance and prevents odors.
