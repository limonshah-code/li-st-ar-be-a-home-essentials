## Adjustable Settings Protect Hand-Knotted Treasures

Oriental rugs often feature intricate hand-knotted designs. These designs are beautiful and valuable. A vacuum's rotating brush roll can be very damaging to these specific rug types. Standard vacuums use stiff brush rolls to agitate carpet fibers. This helps lift dirt. For Oriental rugs, such agitation can pull out loops, fray pile, and even unravel knots. Wreck vacuums address this problem with their adjustable settings. Many Wreck models allow you to turn off the brush roll completely. This is a critical feature for Oriental rugs. In addition to brush roll control, Wreck vacuums offer adjustable height settings. This lets you raise the vacuum head higher above the rug surface. A higher setting reduces direct contact.
