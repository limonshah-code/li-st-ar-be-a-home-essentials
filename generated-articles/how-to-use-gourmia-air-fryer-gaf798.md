### Advanced Tips for Maximizing Your GAF798

To get the most out of your Gourmia GAF798, consider these advanced tips. These practices help optimize cooking results and extend your appliance's life. I have found these make a noticeable difference.

**Shake and Flip Consistently:** For most foods, especially fries or chicken, shaking the basket or flipping items is crucial. This ensures all sides get exposed to the hot air. It leads to uniform crispiness and browning. Do this at least once, often halfway through the cooking time.

**Use Minimal Oil:** Air fryers reduce the need for oil. A light spray or toss with a teaspoon of oil is usually enough. Too much oil can cause smoke and make food greasy. This also keeps your air fryer cleaner.

**Avoid Overcrowding:** This is perhaps the most important tip. Overcrowding stops hot air from circulating. It makes food steam instead of crisp. Cook in smaller batches if needed. This yields superior results.

**Pat Food Dry:** For naturally moist foods like chicken wings or fresh vegetables, patting them dry helps. Excess moisture creates steam. Dry surfaces allow for better browning and crisping.

**Consider Liners:** Parchment paper liners designed for air fryers can make cleanup easier. They catch drips and crumbs. Ensure the liner does not block air circulation. Never preheat with an empty liner, as it could fly up and touch the heating element.

**Adjust for Doneness:** Cooking times are guidelines. Actual times vary based on food thickness, starting temperature, and personal preference. Check food frequently. You can always add a few more minutes if needed. A meat thermometer ensures proper doneness for meats.

**Understand Temperature Conversion:** If a recipe uses Celsius and your GAF798 displays Fahrenheit, or vice versa, know how to convert. This prevents errors. Most Gourmia models allow changing between C and F.

Applying these tips will elevate your air frying skills. Your Gourmia GAF798 will consistently produce delicious, crispy meals. You will enjoy the healthier cooking experience it provides even more.
