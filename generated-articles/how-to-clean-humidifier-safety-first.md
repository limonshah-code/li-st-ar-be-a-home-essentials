## Clean Your Humidifier: Safety First for Healthier Air

Do you enjoy the soothing mist from your humidifier? Many of us appreciate the comfort it brings, especially during dry seasons. A humidifier adds moisture to the air, helping with dry skin, congested sinuses, and even protecting wooden furniture. However, this helpful appliance can quickly become a problem if not cared for properly. Today, I will show you how to clean your humidifier safety first.
