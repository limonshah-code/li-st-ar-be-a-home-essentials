## FAQ Section

### Q1: Can all Chefman air fryer models switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit?

A1: No, not all Chefman air fryer models can switch temperature units. Analog models with physical dials typically display only one unit, usually Fahrenheit, and do not offer a conversion option. Digital models usually have this feature, but the specific method varies. Always check your user manual for exact details regarding your specific Chefman air fryer model.

### Q2: What if my Chefman air fryer doesn't have a 'TEMP' or 'C/F' button?

A2: If your Chefman air fryer lacks a dedicated 'TEMP' or 'C/F' button, try pressing and holding other temperature-related controls. Often, pressing the 'Time' and 'Temperature' adjustment buttons simultaneously for 3-5 seconds works. Sometimes, pressing both the plus (+) and minus (-) temperature arrows together can also trigger the unit change.

### Q3: How long should I hold the button to change the temperature unit?

A3: You typically need to press and hold the specific button or button combination for about 3 to 5 seconds. Some models might require holding it slightly longer, up to 10 seconds. Observe the digital display during this time. The unit change from 'C' to 'F' (or vice-versa) usually happens instantly once the air fryer registers the command.

### Q4: Does changing the temperature unit affect cooking performance?

A4: No, changing the temperature unit from Celsius to Fahrenheit (or vice-versa) only affects how the temperature is displayed. It does not change the air fryer's actual heating performance or cooking capability. The air fryer still operates at the same internal temperature. This setting simply adjusts how that temperature value is shown to the user.

### Q5: Can I reset my Chefman air fryer to factory settings to change the unit?

A5: Some digital appliances have a factory reset option, but it's not a common method for changing temperature units on Chefman air fryers. A simple power cycle (unplugging and plugging back in) might reset some minor settings. The standard method for unit conversion is a specific button press as outlined in the manual, not a full factory reset.

### Q6: Why did my Chefman air fryer suddenly switch to Celsius?

A6: Your Chefman air fryer might switch to Celsius if there was an accidental button press. This can happen if a button is pressed and held for too long during normal operation or cleaning. Power fluctuations can also sometimes cause settings to revert. Simply follow the steps to switch it back to Fahrenheit.
