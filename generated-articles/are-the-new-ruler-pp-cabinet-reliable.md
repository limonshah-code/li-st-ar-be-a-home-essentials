### Understanding Polypropylene: The Core Material

Polypropylene, or PP, is a thermoplastic polymer. Manufacturers create it from propene monomer. This material is known for its ruggedness. PP offers a good balance of strength and flexibility.

The properties of PP make it suitable for cabinets. It resists various impacts well. PP also does not absorb water. This feature is crucial for cabinet performance in wet areas.

Ruler PP cabinets benefit directly from this material. They gain inherent resistance to moisture damage. This makes them a strong choice for specific home needs. The material helps ensure long-term stability and function.
