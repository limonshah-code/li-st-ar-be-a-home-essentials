## Using Dishwasher Magic for Optimal Results

Once you have Dishwasher Magic, using it correctly ensures your dishwasher gets the best clean. Proper application maximizes the product's effectiveness. It helps you maintain a truly spotless appliance. I follow the instructions closely for the best outcome.

### Step-by-Step Application

Using Dishwasher Magic is simple. First, empty your dishwasher completely. Do not run it with dishes inside when cleaning with Dishwasher Magic. Next, remove the cap from the Dishwasher Magic bottle. Do not remove the wax plug. Place the bottle upside down in the bottom rack of your dishwasher. Secure it so it does not tip over during the cycle.

Close the dishwasher door. Then, run a hot water cycle. The hottest setting is usually best for thorough cleaning. The product will dispense and clean the interior during the cycle. After the cycle finishes, remove the empty bottle. Your dishwasher should now be noticeably cleaner and fresher.

### Frequency of Use

## FAQ Section

### How often should I use Dishwasher Magic?
You should use Dishwasher Magic once a month for regular maintenance. If you have very hard water, or use your dishwasher every day, you might consider using it every two to three weeks. Consistent use prevents mineral buildup, odors, and keeps your dishwasher performing optimally.

### Can I use Dishwasher Magic with dishes inside?
No, you should not use Dishwasher Magic with dishes inside your dishwasher. The product is a strong cleaner specifically for the appliance's interior. Always run Dishwasher Magic on an empty dishwasher. This ensures the cleaner targets mineral buildup and grime, not your dishes.

### Is Dishwasher Magic safe for all dishwasher types?
Yes, Dishwasher Magic is generally safe for all dishwasher types, including stainless steel interiors. It is formulated to clean the internal components without causing damage. Always follow the instructions on the bottle for proper and safe use.
