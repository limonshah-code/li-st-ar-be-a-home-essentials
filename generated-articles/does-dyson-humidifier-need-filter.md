These filters often need regular replacement. They can get moldy. They can also collect minerals. If not replaced, they can spread dirty air. Dyson's approach combines elements. It uses UV-C like warm mist humidifiers use heat to kill bacteria. It then disperses mist like an ultrasonic unit.
