### Frequently Asked Questions

No, you cannot return a Zinus mattress after the 100-night sleep trial period ends. The policy requires all return requests to be initiated within this window. #### Do I need the original box to return a Zinus mattress?
No, you do not need the original box to return your Zinus mattress. Zinus understands that mattresses are difficult to re-compress and re-box. They arrange for a third-party service to pick up the mattress from your home. You simply need to ensure the mattress is ready for collection.

#### How long does a Zinus refund take?
A Zinus refund typically takes 5-10 business days to process after the mattress has been picked up and inspected. The funds are returned to your original payment method. Zinus usually sends an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated. Always monitor your bank statement for the credited amount.

#### What if my Zinus mattress is defective?
If your Zinus mattress is defective, it falls under their warranty policy. Contact Zinus customer service immediately. Provide details and photos of the defect. They will assess the issue and may offer a replacement or a refund. This process is separate from the standard 100-night sleep trial for comfort.

#### Can I exchange my Zinus mattress for a different one?
Zinus's policy primarily focuses on returns for a refund. They generally do not offer direct exchanges for a different model. If you wish for a different mattress, you would return your current one for a refund. Then you would place a new order for the mattress you prefer.

#### Is there a fee to return a Zinus mattress?
No, Zinus does not charge a fee for returning a mattress within the 100-night sleep trial period. They cover the cost of pickup and provide a full refund. This policy applies as long as the mattress meets their eligibility criteria for condition and timeframe.
