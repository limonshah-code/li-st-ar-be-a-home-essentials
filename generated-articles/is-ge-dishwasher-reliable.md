## Maintaining Your GE Dishwasher for Longevity

Proper maintenance is key to extending your GE dishwasher's life. Regular care prevents small problems from becoming big ones. It also ensures your dishes always come out sparkling clean. I make sure to do a few simple things to keep my dishwasher in top shape. These steps are easy and take little time.

First, clean the interior regularly. Food particles and grease can build up inside the tub. This buildup can lead to bad odors and poor cleaning performance. Use a dishwasher cleaner specifically designed for appliances. You can also run an empty cycle with white vinegar on the top rack. This helps remove mineral deposits and grime. For detailed steps, consider reading about [how to clean your GE dishwasher](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-your-ge-dishwasher).

### Essential Maintenance Steps

The dishwasher filter is a critical component for clean dishes. It traps food debris and prevents it from recirculating during the wash cycle. A clogged filter reduces water flow and cleaning efficiency. Most GE dishwashers have a removable filter that needs regular cleaning. I recommend checking it at least once a month. Rinse it under running water to remove any trapped particles. If it is very dirty, use a brush and some dish soap. GE dishwashers do have filters, and understanding their function helps with maintenance.

Beyond cleaning, inspect the spray arms. Small holes in the spray arms can get clogged with hard water deposits. This stops water from spraying correctly onto your dishes. Use a small wire or toothpick to clear any blockages. Also, check the door gasket for any tears or cracks. A damaged gasket can cause leaks. Keeping these parts clean and functional helps your GE dishwasher run efficiently for years.
