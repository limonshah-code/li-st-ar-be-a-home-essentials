## Cleaning and Maintenance

Cleaning an air fryer is a crucial part of owning one. Hamilton Beach air fryers are generally easy to clean. Most models come with non-stick baskets and trays. These parts are often dishwasher safe, which simplifies the cleaning process significantly. You can simply place them in the dishwasher after use.

Wiping down the exterior of the unit with a damp cloth is usually enough. Make sure the unit is unplugged and cool before cleaning. ### Tips for Easy Cleaning

Always let the air fryer cool down completely before cleaning any parts. This prevents burns and protects the non-stick surfaces. For stubborn, baked-on food, soak the removable parts in hot, soapy water for a few minutes. This softens the residue.

Use a silicone spatula or a wooden utensil to gently scrape off any stuck-on bits. Avoid metal utensils as they can scratch the non-stick coating. Periodically check the heating element for grease splatter. You can gently wipe it with a damp cloth once it is completely cool and unplugged. Regular cleaning ensures your air fryer continues to function effectively and safely.
