## FAQ Section

### Q1: Can I put my Yeti Rambler bottle in the dishwasher?
Yes, all Yeti Rambler bottles are dishwasher safe. You can safely place the bottle body and its cap on the top rack of your dishwasher. This applies to all sizes and colors of Rambler bottles.

### Q2: Will dishwashing damage the DuraCoat color finish on my Yeti cup?
No, Yeti's DuraCoat color finish is highly durable and designed to resist fading, peeling, and cracking even with regular dishwashing. The finish is engineered to withstand the conditions of a typical dishwasher cycle.

### Q3: Do I need to remove the MagSlider from my Yeti lid before dishwashing?
You do not need to remove the MagSlider for general dishwashing. However, for a more thorough clean, especially if you notice residue buildup, removing the MagSlider magnet allows you to clean under it effectively by hand.

### Q4: Is it better to hand wash or machine wash my Yeti coffee cup?
Both hand washing and machine washing are effective. Dishwashing offers convenience for everyday cleaning. Hand washing allows for targeted cleaning of stubborn stains or hard-to-reach areas. You can use both methods as needed.

### Q5: Can I put older Yeti models in the dishwasher?
Most older Yeti Rambler drinkware is also dishwasher safe. However, for specific older models or unique accessories, always refer to the care instructions that came with your product or check Yeti's official website for definitive guidance.

### Q6: What if my Yeti coffee cup starts to smell or has stains that won't come out?
For persistent odors or stains, try hand washing with warm, soapy water and a baking soda paste. Mix baking soda with a little water to form a paste, apply it to the stain, gently scrub, and rinse thoroughly.
