## Removing Your Roborock Washable Filter: A Simple Guide

Your Roborock robot vacuum is a smart helper for keeping floors clean. It picks up dust, dirt, and pet hair efficiently. For it to work its best, regular maintenance is important. A key part of this is knowing how to remove the washable filter from your Roborock. This filter traps small particles. Keeping it clean ensures your vacuum maintains strong suction. It also helps your home stay free of allergens. This guide shows you each step to properly remove and care for your Roborock's washable filter.

### Takeaway:

*   Regular filter removal is important for vacuum performance.
*   Locate the dustbin on your specific Roborock model.
*   Open the dustbin, then access and unclip the filter.
*   Clean the filter according to Roborock guidelines.
*   Ensure the filter is completely dry before reinserting.

**To remove the washable filter from your Roborock, first open the main lid or dustbin cover of your vacuum. Then, carefully lift out the dustbin. Once the dustbin is separate, unclip or slide the filter out from its slot. This process varies slightly by Roborock model but generally involves simple, tool-free steps.**
