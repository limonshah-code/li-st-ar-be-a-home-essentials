## Keeping It Clean: How To Effectively Clean Your Keyboard Cover

Has your keyboard cover lost its clear look? A dirty keyboard cover collects dust, crumbs, and oils from your fingers. This can make your keyboard look unkempt and harbor germs. Cleaning your keyboard cover regularly helps maintain a hygienic workspace and keeps your devices looking new.

You protect your keyboard with a cover, but what about the cover itself? Over time, these protective layers accumulate dirt and grime, diminishing their effectiveness and appearance.
