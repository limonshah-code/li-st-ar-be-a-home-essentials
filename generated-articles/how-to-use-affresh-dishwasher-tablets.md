### Preparing Your Dishwasher for an Affresh Clean

Before you use an Affresh Dishwasher Tablet, a little preparation helps. This ensures the tablet can work most effectively. The goal is to let the cleaner focus on machine grime, not food scraps. Taking a few moments to prepare ensures a truly deep clean. This step is simple but important for good results.

I always tell people to start with an empty dishwasher. This is a common mistake I see. Many think they can clean dishes and the machine at the same time. This is not how Affresh works. It is a machine cleaner, not a dish cleaner. Proper preparation makes the cleaning cycle count.

#### Emptying the Appliance

First, remove all dishes, cutlery, and racks from your dishwasher. Yes, this means completely empty. The Affresh tablet needs direct access to all internal surfaces. It needs to flow freely around the spray arms and through the filter. If dishes are present, they will block the cleaning solution. The solution will then clean your dishes instead of the machine. This wastes the tablet and does not clean your dishwasher properly. Make sure the utensil basket is also empty. Check every corner for stray items.

While the dishwasher is empty, quickly inspect the interior. Look for any large food particles. You might find pieces of food stuck in the bottom. Wipe these away with a paper towel. This prevents them from simply circulating during the cleaning cycle. Also, check your dishwasher filter. Many dishwashers have a removable filter at the bottom. This filter traps larger food debris. If it is visibly clogged, remove it and rinse it under hot water. You do not need to do a full filter clean every time. But a quick check ensures water flows freely. ### Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use Affresh Dishwasher Tablets

Using Affresh Dishwasher Tablets is simple. Follow these steps for the best cleaning results. Proper placement and cycle selection are key. Do not rush this process. A few simple actions ensure your dishwasher gets a thorough clean. I have found this method consistently provides excellent outcomes.

Many people wonder where to put the tablet. It is not as complex as some make it out to be. Just think about where your regular detergent goes. Affresh goes in the same spot.

#### Correct Placement of the Tablet

Locate your dishwasher's main detergent dispenser. This is usually a small compartment on the inside of the dishwasher door. Open the compartment. Place one Affresh Dishwasher Tablet directly inside this dispenser. Close the dispenser door. Some people think you should put it in the bottom of the tub. Do not do this. Placing it in the dispenser ensures the tablet dissolves at the correct time during the wash cycle. This allows the cleaning agents to activate properly.

You might be wondering about the tablet's wrapper. Many dishwasher tablets come with a dissolvable film. Affresh Dishwasher Tablets are designed to be unwrapped before use, even if it looks like there's a film. Always check the package directions. Most Affresh tablets require you to remove the plastic wrapper. This ensures the tablet dissolves completely. #### Choosing the Right Cycle

After placing the tablet, select the right wash cycle. For best results, choose a "Normal" or "Heavy" wash cycle. Make sure the cycle includes a hot water wash. Some dishwashers have a "Sanitize" or "Hot Wash" option. These are excellent choices. The hot water helps activate the cleaning agents in the tablet. It also helps break down grease and dissolve mineral deposits. Avoid quick or light wash cycles. They might not provide enough time or hot water for a thorough clean.

#### Running the Cleaning Cycle

Once you have selected the cycle, start your dishwasher. Let the full cycle complete. During the cycle, the Affresh tablet will dissolve. It will release its cleaning agents throughout the dishwasher's interior. You might hear normal washing sounds. There is no need to monitor it constantly. Just let the machine do its work.

After the cycle finishes, open the dishwasher door. You should notice a clean, fresh smell. The interior surfaces should look brighter and cleaner. You can then resume using your dishwasher as usual for dishes. This simple process can revive an underperforming appliance.

### Common Mistakes to Avoid When Using Affresh

Using Affresh Dishwasher Tablets is straightforward. But some common mistakes can reduce their effectiveness. Knowing these pitfalls helps you get the most from each cleaning cycle. I have seen these errors often. Avoiding them saves you time and ensures a cleaner machine.

It is easy to assume all dishwasher products work the same. However, Affresh is unique. It is specifically for the appliance itself. Understanding this difference is important.

#### Do Not Combine with Dishes

The most common mistake is running Affresh with dishes inside. Affresh is a machine cleaner. It is designed to clean the internal parts of the dishwasher, not your tableware. The chemicals are formulated for stubborn machine residue, not food on dishes. Using Affresh with dishes can leave a film on them. It can also prevent the tablet from properly cleaning the machine. Always ensure your dishwasher is completely empty before running a cleaning cycle with Affresh. This guarantees the tablet's full power goes towards cleaning the appliance.

#### Avoiding Dispenser Errors

Some people place the Affresh tablet directly in the bottom of the dishwasher tub. This is incorrect. The tablet needs to go into the main detergent dispenser. This dispenser releases the cleaning agent at the optimal time during the cycle. Placing it in the tub allows it to dissolve too early. This reduces its contact time with the full wash cycle. Always open the detergent compartment and place the tablet inside. Then close the dispenser door firmly. This simple step ensures correct activation and distribution of the cleaning solution.

Another error is not removing the wrapper if the tablet has one. While many dishwasher tablets for dishes have dissolvable wrappers, some Affresh tablets do not. Always check the packaging. If it says to unwrap, then remove the plastic. Leaving a non-dissolvable wrapper on will prevent the tablet from working. It will not dissolve properly, leaving a messy residue. I always double-check this detail myself to avoid issues.

If you see clogged holes, use a toothpick or small wire to clear them. This ensures proper water distribution during cycles.

Another tip is to leave the dishwasher door ajar after each cycle. This allows air to circulate. It helps dry the interior. This prevents mold and mildew growth. It also stops unpleasant odors from developing between washes. Good airflow is simple but effective for freshness.

Use the correct amount of dish detergent. Too much detergent can leave residue on dishes and inside the machine. Too little means dishes do not get clean. Read your detergent's instructions.
